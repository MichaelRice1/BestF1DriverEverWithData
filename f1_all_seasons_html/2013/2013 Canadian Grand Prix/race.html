The 2013 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 9, 2013 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 09 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2013 Canadian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 132 points. Vettel is 36 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
