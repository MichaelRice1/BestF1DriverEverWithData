The 2013 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 28, 2013 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Romain Grosjean started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 29 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2013 Hungarian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 172 points. Vettel is 38 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
