The 2013 Korean Grand Prix started Oct 6, 2013 on Korea International Circuit in Yeongam, South Korea. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 43 minutes and 13 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Romain Grosjean in third place.

After the 2013 Korean Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 272 points. Vettel is 77 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
