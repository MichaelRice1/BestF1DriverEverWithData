The 2013 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 3, 2013 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Mark Webber started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 06 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Nico Rosberg in third place.

After the 2013 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 347 points. Vettel is 130 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
