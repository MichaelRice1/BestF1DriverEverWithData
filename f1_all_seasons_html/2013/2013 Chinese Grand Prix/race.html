The 2013 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 14, 2013 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 26 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2013 Chinese Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 52 points. Vettel is 3 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
