The 2013 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 22, 2013 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Romain Grosjean started from third place.

In 1 hour, 59 minutes and 13 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2013 Singapore Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 247 points. Vettel is 60 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
