The 2013 Spanish Grand Prix started May 12, 2013 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 16 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2013 Spanish Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 89 points. Vettel is 4 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
