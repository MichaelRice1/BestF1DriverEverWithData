The 2013 United States Grand Prix started Nov 17, 2013 on Circuit of the Americas in Austin, United States of America. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Romain Grosjean started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 17 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Romain Grosjean in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2013 United States Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 372 points. Vettel is 145 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
