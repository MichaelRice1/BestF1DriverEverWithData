The 2013 Brazilian Grand Prix started Nov 24, 2013 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 36 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2013 Brazilian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 397 points. Vettel is 155 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
