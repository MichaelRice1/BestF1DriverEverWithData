The 2013 Indian Grand Prix started Oct 27, 2013 on Buddh International Circuit in Greater Noida, India. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 12 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Romain Grosjean in third place.

After the 2013 Indian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 322 points. Vettel is 115 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
