The 2013 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 8, 2013 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Nico Hülkenberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 18 minutes and 33 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2013 Italian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 222 points. Vettel is 53 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
