The 2017 Malaysian Grand Prix started Oct 1, 2017 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 01 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2017 Malaysian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 281 points. Hamilton is 34 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
