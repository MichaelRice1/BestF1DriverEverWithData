The 2017 United States Grand Prix started Oct 22, 2017 on Circuit of the Americas in Austin, United States of America. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 50 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2017 United States Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 331 points. Hamilton is 66 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
