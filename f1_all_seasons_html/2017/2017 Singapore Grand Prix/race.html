The 2017 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 17, 2017 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 2 hour, 03 minutes and 23 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Daniel Ricciardo in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2017 Singapore Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 263 points. Hamilton is 28 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
