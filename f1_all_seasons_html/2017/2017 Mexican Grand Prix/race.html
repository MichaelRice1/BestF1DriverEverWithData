The 2017 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 29, 2017 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 26 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2017 Mexican Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 333 points. Hamilton is 56 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
