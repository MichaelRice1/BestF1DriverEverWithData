The 1987 Detroit Grand Prix started Jun 21, 1987 on Detroit Street Circuit in Detroit, United States of America. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 16 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1987 Detroit Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Senna is 2 points ahead of Alain Prost.
