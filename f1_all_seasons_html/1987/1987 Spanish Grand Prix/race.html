The 1987 Spanish Grand Prix started Sep 27, 1987 on Circuito de Jerez in Jerez de la Frontera, Spain. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 12 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Stefan Johansson in third place.

After the 1987 Spanish Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Piquet is 18 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
