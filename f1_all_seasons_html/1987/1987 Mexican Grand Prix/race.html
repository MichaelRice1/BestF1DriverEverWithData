The 1987 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 18, 1987 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 24 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1987 Mexican Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 73 points. Piquet is 12 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
