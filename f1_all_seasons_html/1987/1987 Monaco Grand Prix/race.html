The 1987 Monaco Grand Prix started May 31, 1987 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 57 minutes and 54 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Michele Alboreto in third place.

After the 1987 Monaco Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Prost is 3 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
