The 1987 San Marino Grand Prix started May 3, 1987 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 24 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Michele Alboreto in third place.

After the 1987 San Marino Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Mansell is 1 points ahead of Alain Prost.
