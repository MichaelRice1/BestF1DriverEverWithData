The 2001 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 18, 2001 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Ralf Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 47 minutes and 34 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2001 Malaysian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Schumacher is 10 points ahead of David Coulthard.
