The 2001 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 4, 2001 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 26 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2001 Australian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Schumacher is 4 points ahead of David Coulthard.
