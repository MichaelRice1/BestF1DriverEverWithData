The 2001 European Grand Prix started Jun 24, 2001 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Ralf Schumacher next to him. Juan Pablo Montoya started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 42 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2001 European Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 68 points. Schumacher is 24 points ahead of David Coulthard.
