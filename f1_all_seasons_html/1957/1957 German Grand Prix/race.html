The 1957 German Grand Prix started Aug 4, 1957 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Juan Manuel Fangio started from pole position with Mike Hawthorn next to him. Jean Behra started from third place.

In 3 hour, 30 minutes and 38 seconds Juan Manuel Fangio finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mike Hawthorn in second and Peter Collins in third place.

After the 1957 German Grand Prix, Juan Manuel Fangio is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 34 points. Fangio is 18 points ahead of Luigi Musso.
