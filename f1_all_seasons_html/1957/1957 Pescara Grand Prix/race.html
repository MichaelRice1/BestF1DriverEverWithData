The 1957 Pescara Grand Prix started Aug 18, 1957 on Pescara Circuit in Pescara, Italy. Juan Manuel Fangio started from pole position with Stirling Moss next to him. Luigi Musso started from third place.

In 2 hour, 59 minutes and 22 seconds Stirling Moss finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Manuel Fangio in second and Harry Schell in third place.

After the 1957 Pescara Grand Prix, Juan Manuel Fangio is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Fangio is 23 points ahead of Stirling Moss.
