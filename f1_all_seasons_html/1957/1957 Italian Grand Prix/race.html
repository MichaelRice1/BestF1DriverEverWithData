The 1957 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 8, 1957 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Stuart Lewis-Evans started from pole position with Stirling Moss next to him. Tony Brooks started from third place.

In 2 hour, 35 minutes and 03 seconds Stirling Moss finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Manuel Fangio in second and Wolfgang von Trips in third place.

After the 1957 Italian Grand Prix, Juan Manuel Fangio is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Fangio is 15 points ahead of Stirling Moss.
