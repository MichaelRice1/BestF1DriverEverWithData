The 2008 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 3, 2008 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Heikki Kovalainen next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 27 seconds Heikki Kovalainen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Timo Glock in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2008 Hungarian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 62 points. Hamilton is 5 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
