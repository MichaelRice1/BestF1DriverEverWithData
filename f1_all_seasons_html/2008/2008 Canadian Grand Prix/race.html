The 2008 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 8, 2008 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Robert Kubica next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 24 seconds Robert Kubica finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nick Heidfeld in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2008 Canadian Grand Prix, Robert Kubica is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 42 points. Kubica is 4 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
