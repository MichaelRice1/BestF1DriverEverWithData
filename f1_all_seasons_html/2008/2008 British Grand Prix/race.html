The 2008 British Grand Prix started Jul 6, 2008 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Heikki Kovalainen started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 09 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nick Heidfeld in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2008 British Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 48 points. Hamilton is 0 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
