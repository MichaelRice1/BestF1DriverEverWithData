The 2008 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 6, 2008 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Robert Kubica started from pole position with Felipe Massa next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 06 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Robert Kubica in third place.

After the 2008 Bahrain Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 19 points. Räikkönen is 3 points ahead of Nick Heidfeld.
