The 2008 Belgian Grand Prix started Sep 7, 2008 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Felipe Massa next to him. Heikki Kovalainen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 22 minutes and 59 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nick Heidfeld in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2008 Belgian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Hamilton is 2 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
