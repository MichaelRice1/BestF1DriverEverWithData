The 2008 Brazilian Grand Prix started Nov 2, 2008 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Jarno Trulli next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 11 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2008 Brazilian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 98 points. Hamilton is 1 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
