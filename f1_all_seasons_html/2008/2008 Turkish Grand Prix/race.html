The 2008 Turkish Grand Prix started May 11, 2008 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Heikki Kovalainen next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 49 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2008 Turkish Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 35 points. Räikkönen is 7 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
