The 2008 European Grand Prix started Aug 24, 2008 on Valencia Street Circuit in Valencia, Spain. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Robert Kubica started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 32 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Robert Kubica in third place.

After the 2008 European Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Hamilton is 6 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
