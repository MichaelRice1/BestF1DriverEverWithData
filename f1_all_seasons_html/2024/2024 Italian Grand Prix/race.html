The 2024 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 1, 2024 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Lando Norris started from pole position with Oscar Piastri next to him. George Russell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 14 minutes and 40 seconds Charles Leclerc finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Oscar Piastri in second and Lando Norris in third place.

After the 2024 Italian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 303 points. Verstappen is 62 points ahead of Lando Norris.
