The 2024 Qatar Grand Prix started Dec 1, 2024 on Losail International Circuit in Lusail, Qatar. Max Verstappen started from pole position with George Russell next to him. Lando Norris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 05 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Oscar Piastri in third place.

After the 2024 Qatar Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 429 points. Verstappen is 80 points ahead of Lando Norris.
