The 2024 Miami Grand Prix started May 5, 2024 on Miami International Autodrome in Miami Gardens, United States of America. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 49 seconds Lando Norris finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2024 Miami Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 136 points. Verstappen is 33 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
