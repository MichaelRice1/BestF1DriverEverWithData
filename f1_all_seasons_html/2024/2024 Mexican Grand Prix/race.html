The 2024 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 27, 2024 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Lando Norris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 55 seconds Carlos Sainz Jr. finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2024 Mexican Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 362 points. Verstappen is 47 points ahead of Lando Norris.
