The 2024 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 21, 2024 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 52 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2024 Chinese Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 110 points. Verstappen is 25 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
