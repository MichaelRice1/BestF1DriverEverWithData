The 2024 Austrian Grand Prix started Jun 30, 2024 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Lando Norris next to him. George Russell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 22 seconds George Russell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Oscar Piastri in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2024 Austrian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 237 points. Verstappen is 81 points ahead of Lando Norris.
