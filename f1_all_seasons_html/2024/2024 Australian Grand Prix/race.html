The 2024 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 24, 2024 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Carlos Sainz Jr. next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 20 minutes and 26 seconds Carlos Sainz Jr. finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Lando Norris in third place.

After the 2024 Australian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 51 points. Verstappen is 4 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
