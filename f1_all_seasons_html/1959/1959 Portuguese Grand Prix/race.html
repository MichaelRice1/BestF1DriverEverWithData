The 1959 Portuguese Grand Prix started Aug 23, 1959 on Circuito de Monsanto in Lisbon, Portugal. Stirling Moss started from pole position with Jack Brabham next to him. Masten Gregory started from third place.

In 2 hour, 11 minutes and 55 seconds Stirling Moss finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Masten Gregory in second and Dan Gurney in third place.

After the 1959 Portuguese Grand Prix, Jack Brabham is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 27 points. Brabham is 4 points ahead of Tony Brooks.
