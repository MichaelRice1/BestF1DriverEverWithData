The 2018 Azerbaijan Grand Prix started Apr 29, 2018 on Baku City Circuit in Baku, Azerbaijan. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 43 minutes and 44 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2018 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Hamilton is 4 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
