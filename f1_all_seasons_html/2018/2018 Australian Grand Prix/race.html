The 2018 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 25, 2018 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 33 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2018 Australian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 25 points. Vettel is 7 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
