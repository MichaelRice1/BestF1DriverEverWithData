The 2018 Spanish Grand Prix started May 13, 2018 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 29 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2018 Spanish Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 95 points. Hamilton is 17 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
