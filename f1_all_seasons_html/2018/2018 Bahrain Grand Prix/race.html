The 2018 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 8, 2018 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 01 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2018 Bahrain Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 50 points. Vettel is 17 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
