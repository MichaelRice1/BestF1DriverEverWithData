The 1993 European Grand Prix started Apr 11, 1993 on Donington Park in Leicestershire, United Kingdom. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 46 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1993 European Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 26 points. Senna is 12 points ahead of Alain Prost.
