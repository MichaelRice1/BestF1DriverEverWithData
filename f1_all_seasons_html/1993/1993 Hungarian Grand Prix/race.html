The 1993 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 15, 1993 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 47 minutes and 39 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1993 Hungarian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 77 points. Prost is 27 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
