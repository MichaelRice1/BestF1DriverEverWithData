The 1993 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 29, 1993 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 32 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1993 Belgian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 81 points. Prost is 28 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
