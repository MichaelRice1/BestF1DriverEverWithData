The 1993 British Grand Prix started Jul 11, 1993 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 25 minutes and 38 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1993 British Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 67 points. Prost is 20 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
