The 1993 German Grand Prix started Jul 25, 1993 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 18 minutes and 40 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Mark Blundell in third place.

After the 1993 German Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 77 points. Prost is 27 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
