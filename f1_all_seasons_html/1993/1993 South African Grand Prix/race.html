The 1993 South African Grand Prix started Mar 14, 1993 on Kyalami Racing Circuit in Midrand, South Africa. Alain Prost started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 45 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Mark Blundell in third place.

After the 1993 South African Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Prost is 4 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
