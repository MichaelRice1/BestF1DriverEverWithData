The 2003 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 9, 2003 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Juan Pablo Montoya started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 42 seconds David Coulthard finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2003 Australian Grand Prix, David Coulthard is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Coulthard is 2 points ahead of Juan Pablo Montoya.
