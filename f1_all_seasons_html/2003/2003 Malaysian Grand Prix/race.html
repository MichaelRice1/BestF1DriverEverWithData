The 2003 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 23, 2003 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Jarno Trulli next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 22 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2003 Malaysian Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 16 points. Räikkönen is 6 points ahead of David Coulthard.
