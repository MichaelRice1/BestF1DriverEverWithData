The 2003 German Grand Prix started Aug 3, 2003 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Juan Pablo Montoya started from pole position with Ralf Schumacher next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 48 seconds Juan Pablo Montoya finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Jarno Trulli in third place.

After the 2003 German Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 71 points. Schumacher is 6 points ahead of Juan Pablo Montoya.
