The 2003 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 12, 2003 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Rubens Barrichello started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Cristiano da Matta started from third place.

In 1 hour, 25 minutes and 11 seconds Rubens Barrichello finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2003 Japanese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 93 points. Schumacher is 2 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
