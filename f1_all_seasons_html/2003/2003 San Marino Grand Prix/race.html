The 2003 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 20, 2003 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Ralf Schumacher next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 12 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2003 San Marino Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 32 points. Räikkönen is 13 points ahead of David Coulthard.
