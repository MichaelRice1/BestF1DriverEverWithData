The 1975 Spanish Grand Prix started Apr 27, 1975 on Circuito de Montjuïc in Barcelona, Spain. Niki Lauda started from pole position with Clay Regazzoni next to him. James Hunt started from third place.

In 42 minutes and 53 seconds Jochen Mass finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacky Ickx in second and Carlos Reutemann in third place.

After the 1975 Spanish Grand Prix, Emerson Fittipaldi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Fittipaldi is 3 points ahead of Carlos Pace.
