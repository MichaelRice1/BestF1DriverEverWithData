The 1975 Argentine Grand Prix started Jan 12, 1975 on Autódromo Juan y Oscar Gálvez in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Jean-Pierre Jarier started from pole position with Carlos Pace next to him. Carlos Reutemann started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 26 seconds Emerson Fittipaldi finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with James Hunt in second and Carlos Reutemann in third place.

After the 1975 Argentine Grand Prix, Emerson Fittipaldi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Fittipaldi is 3 points ahead of James Hunt.
