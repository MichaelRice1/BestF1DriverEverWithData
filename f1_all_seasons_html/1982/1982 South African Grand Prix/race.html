The 1982 South African Grand Prix started Jan 23, 1982 on Kyalami Racing Circuit in Midrand, South Africa. René Arnoux started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Gilles Villeneuve started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 08 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Reutemann in second and René Arnoux in third place.

After the 1982 South African Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Prost is 3 points ahead of Carlos Reutemann.
