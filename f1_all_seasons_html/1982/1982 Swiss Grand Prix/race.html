The 1982 Swiss Grand Prix started Aug 29, 1982 on Dijon-Prenois in Dijon, France. Alain Prost started from pole position with René Arnoux next to him. Riccardo Patrese started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 41 seconds Keke Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Niki Lauda in third place.

After the 1982 Swiss Grand Prix, Keke Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 42 points. Rosberg is 3 points ahead of Didier Pironi.
