The 1982 United States Grand Prix West started Apr 4, 1982 on Long Beach in Long Beach, United States of America. Andrea de Cesaris started from pole position with Niki Lauda next to him. René Arnoux started from third place.

In 1 hour, 58 minutes and 25 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Keke Rosberg in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1982 United States Grand Prix West, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Prost is 6 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
