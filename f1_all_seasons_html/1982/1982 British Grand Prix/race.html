The 1982 British Grand Prix started Jul 18, 1982 on Brands Hatch in Fawkham, United Kingdom. Keke Rosberg started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 33 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Didier Pironi in second and Patrick Tambay in third place.

After the 1982 British Grand Prix, Didier Pironi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 35 points. Pironi is 5 points ahead of John Watson.
