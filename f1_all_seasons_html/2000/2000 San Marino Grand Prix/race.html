The 2000 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 9, 2000 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 39 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mika Häkkinen in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2000 San Marino Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Schumacher is 21 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
