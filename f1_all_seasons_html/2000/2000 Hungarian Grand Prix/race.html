The 2000 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 13, 2000 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 45 minutes and 33 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2000 Hungarian Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 64 points. Häkkinen is 2 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
