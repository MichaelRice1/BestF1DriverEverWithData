The 2000 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 26, 2000 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 35 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Giancarlo Fisichella in second and Heinz-Harald Frentzen in third place.

After the 2000 Brazilian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Schumacher is 12 points ahead of Giancarlo Fisichella.
