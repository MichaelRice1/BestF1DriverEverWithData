The 2000 Malaysian Grand Prix started Oct 22, 2000 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mika Häkkinen next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 54 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2000 Malaysian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 108 points. Schumacher is 19 points ahead of Mika Häkkinen.
