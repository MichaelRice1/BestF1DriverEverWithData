The 2000 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 27, 2000 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Jarno Trulli next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 14 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 2000 Belgian Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 74 points. Häkkinen is 6 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
