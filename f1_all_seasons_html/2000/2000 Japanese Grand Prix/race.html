The 2000 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 8, 2000 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mika Häkkinen next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 53 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mika Häkkinen in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2000 Japanese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 98 points. Schumacher is 12 points ahead of Mika Häkkinen.
