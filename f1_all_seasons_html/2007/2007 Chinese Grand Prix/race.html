The 2007 Chinese Grand Prix started Oct 7, 2007 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 58 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2007 Chinese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 107 points. Hamilton is 4 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
