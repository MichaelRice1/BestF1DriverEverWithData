The 2007 Turkish Grand Prix started Aug 26, 2007 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 42 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2007 Turkish Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 84 points. Hamilton is 5 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
