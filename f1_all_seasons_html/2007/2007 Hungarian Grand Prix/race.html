The 2007 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 5, 2007 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Nick Heidfeld started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 52 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Nick Heidfeld in third place.

After the 2007 Hungarian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 80 points. Hamilton is 7 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
