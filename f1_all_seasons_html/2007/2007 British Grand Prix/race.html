The 2007 British Grand Prix started Jul 8, 2007 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 21 minutes and 43 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2007 British Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Hamilton is 12 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
