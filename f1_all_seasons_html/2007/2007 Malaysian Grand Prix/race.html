The 2007 Malaysian Grand Prix started Apr 8, 2007 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 14 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2007 Malaysian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Alonso is 2 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
