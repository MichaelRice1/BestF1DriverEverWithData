The 2007 European Grand Prix started Jul 22, 2007 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 2 hour, 06 minutes and 26 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Felipe Massa in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2007 European Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Hamilton is 2 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
