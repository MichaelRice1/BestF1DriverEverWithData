The 2007 United States Grand Prix started Jun 17, 2007 on Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Indianapolis, United States of America. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 09 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2007 United States Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 58 points. Hamilton is 10 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
