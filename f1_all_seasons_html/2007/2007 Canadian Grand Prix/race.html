The 2007 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 10, 2007 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Nick Heidfeld started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 11 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nick Heidfeld in second and Alexander Wurz in third place.

After the 2007 Canadian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 48 points. Hamilton is 8 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
