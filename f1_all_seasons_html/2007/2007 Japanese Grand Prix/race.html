The 2007 Japanese Grand Prix started Sep 30, 2007 on Fuji Speedway in Oyama, Japan. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 34 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Heikki Kovalainen in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2007 Japanese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 107 points. Hamilton is 12 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
