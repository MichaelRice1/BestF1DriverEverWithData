The 2007 Brazilian Grand Prix started Oct 21, 2007 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 15 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Felipe Massa in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2007 Brazilian Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 110 points. Räikkönen is 1 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
