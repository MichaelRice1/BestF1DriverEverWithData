The 2022 Monaco Grand Prix started May 29, 2022 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Carlos Sainz Jr. next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 56 minutes and 30 seconds Sergio Pérez finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Sainz Jr. in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2022 Monaco Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 125 points. Verstappen is 9 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
