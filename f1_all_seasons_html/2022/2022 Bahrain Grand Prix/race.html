The 2022 Bahrain Grand Prix started Mar 20, 2022 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 33 seconds Charles Leclerc finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Sainz Jr. in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2022 Bahrain Grand Prix, Charles Leclerc is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 26 points. Leclerc is 8 points ahead of Carlos Sainz Jr..
