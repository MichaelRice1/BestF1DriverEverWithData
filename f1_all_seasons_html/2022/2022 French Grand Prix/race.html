The 2022 French Grand Prix started Jul 24, 2022 on Circuit Paul Ricard in Le Castellet, France. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 02 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2022 French Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 233 points. Verstappen is 63 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
