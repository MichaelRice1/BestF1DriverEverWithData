The 2022 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 9, 2022 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 3 hour, 01 minutes and 44 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2022 Japanese Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 366 points. Verstappen is 113 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
