The 2022 Miami Grand Prix started May 8, 2022 on Miami International Autodrome in Miami Gardens, United States of America. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Carlos Sainz Jr. next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 24 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2022 Miami Grand Prix, Charles Leclerc is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 104 points. Leclerc is 19 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
