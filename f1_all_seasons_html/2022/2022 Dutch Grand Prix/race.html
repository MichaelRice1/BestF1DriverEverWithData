The 2022 Dutch Grand Prix started Sep 4, 2022 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 42 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with George Russell in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2022 Dutch Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 310 points. Verstappen is 109 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
