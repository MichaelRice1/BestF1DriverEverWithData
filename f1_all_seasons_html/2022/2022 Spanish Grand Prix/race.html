The 2022 Spanish Grand Prix started May 22, 2022 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 20 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2022 Spanish Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 110 points. Verstappen is 6 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
