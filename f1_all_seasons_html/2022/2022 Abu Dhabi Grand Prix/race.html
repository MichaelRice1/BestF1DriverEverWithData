The 2022 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 20, 2022 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Charles Leclerc started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 45 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2022 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 454 points. Verstappen is 146 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
