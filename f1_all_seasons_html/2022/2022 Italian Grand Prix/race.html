The 2022 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 11, 2022 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 20 minutes and 27 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2022 Italian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 335 points. Verstappen is 116 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
