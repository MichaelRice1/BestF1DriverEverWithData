The 2022 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 31, 2022 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. George Russell started from pole position with Carlos Sainz Jr. next to him. Charles Leclerc started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 35 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2022 Hungarian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 258 points. Verstappen is 80 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
