The 1976 British Grand Prix started Jul 18, 1976 on Brands Hatch in Fawkham, United Kingdom. Niki Lauda started from pole position with James Hunt next to him. Mario Andretti started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 19 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jody Scheckter in second and John Watson in third place.

After the 1976 British Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 61 points. Lauda is 31 points ahead of Jody Scheckter.
