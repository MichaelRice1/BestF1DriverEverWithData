The 1976 French Grand Prix started Jul 4, 1976 on Circuit Paul Ricard in Le Castellet, France. James Hunt started from pole position with Niki Lauda next to him. Patrick Depailler started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 58 seconds James Hunt finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Depailler in second and John Watson in third place.

After the 1976 French Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 52 points. Lauda is 26 points ahead of James Hunt.
