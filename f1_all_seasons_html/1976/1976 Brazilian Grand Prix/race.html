The 1976 Brazilian Grand Prix started Jan 25, 1976 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. James Hunt started from pole position with Niki Lauda next to him. Jean-Pierre Jarier started from third place.

In 1 hour, 45 minutes and 16 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Depailler in second and Tom Pryce in third place.

After the 1976 Brazilian Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Lauda is 3 points ahead of Patrick Depailler.
