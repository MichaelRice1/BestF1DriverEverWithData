The 2011 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 31, 2011 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 42 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2011 Hungarian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 234 points. Vettel is 85 points ahead of Mark Webber.
