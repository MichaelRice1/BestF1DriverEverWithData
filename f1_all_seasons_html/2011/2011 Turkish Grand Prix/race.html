The 2011 Turkish Grand Prix started May 8, 2011 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 17 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2011 Turkish Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 93 points. Vettel is 34 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
