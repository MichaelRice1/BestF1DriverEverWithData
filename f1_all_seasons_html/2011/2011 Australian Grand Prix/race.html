The 2011 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 27, 2011 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 30 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Vitaly Petrov in third place.

After the 2011 Australian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 25 points. Vettel is 7 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
