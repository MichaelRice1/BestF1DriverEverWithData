The 2011 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 28, 2011 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 44 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2011 Belgian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 259 points. Vettel is 92 points ahead of Mark Webber.
