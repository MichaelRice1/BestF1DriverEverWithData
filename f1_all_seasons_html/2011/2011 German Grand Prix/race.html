The 2011 German Grand Prix started Jul 24, 2011 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Mark Webber started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 30 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2011 German Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 216 points. Vettel is 77 points ahead of Mark Webber.
