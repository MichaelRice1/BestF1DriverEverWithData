The 2011 Spanish Grand Prix started May 22, 2011 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Mark Webber started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 03 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2011 Spanish Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 118 points. Vettel is 41 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
