The 1958 German Grand Prix started Aug 3, 1958 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Mike Hawthorn started from pole position with Tony Brooks next to him. Stirling Moss started from third place.

In 2 hour, 21 minutes and 15 seconds Tony Brooks finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Roy Salvadori in second and Maurice Trintignant in third place.

After the 1958 German Grand Prix, Mike Hawthorn is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Hawthorn is 6 points ahead of Stirling Moss.
