The 1962 German Grand Prix started Aug 5, 1962 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Dan Gurney started from pole position with Graham Hill next to him. Jim Clark started from third place.

In 2 hour, 38 minutes and 45 seconds Graham Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with John Surtees in second and Dan Gurney in third place.

After the 1962 German Grand Prix, Graham Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 28 points. Hill is 7 points ahead of Jim Clark.
