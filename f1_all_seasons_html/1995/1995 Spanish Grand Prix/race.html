The 1995 Spanish Grand Prix started May 14, 1995 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Jean Alesi next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 20 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Johnny Herbert in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1995 Spanish Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Schumacher is 1 points ahead of Damon Hill.
