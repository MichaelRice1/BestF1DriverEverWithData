The 1995 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 27, 1995 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Jean Alesi next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 47 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Martin Brundle in third place.

After the 1995 Belgian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 66 points. Schumacher is 15 points ahead of Damon Hill.
