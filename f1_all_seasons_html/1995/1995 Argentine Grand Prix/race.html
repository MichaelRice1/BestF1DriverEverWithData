The 1995 Argentine Grand Prix started Apr 9, 1995 on Autódromo Juan y Oscar Gálvez in Buenos Aires, Argentina. David Coulthard started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 53 minutes and 14 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1995 Argentine Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 14 points. Schumacher is 4 points ahead of Damon Hill.
