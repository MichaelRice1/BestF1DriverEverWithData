The 1995 European Grand Prix started Oct 1, 1995 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. David Coulthard started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 59 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 1995 European Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 82 points. Schumacher is 27 points ahead of Damon Hill.
