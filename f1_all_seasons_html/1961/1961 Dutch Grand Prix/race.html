The 1961 Dutch Grand Prix started May 22, 1961 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Phil Hill started from pole position with Wolfgang von Trips next to him. Richie Ginther started from third place.

In 2 hour, 01 minutes and 52 seconds Wolfgang von Trips finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Phil Hill in second and Jim Clark in third place.

After the 1961 Dutch Grand Prix, Stirling Moss is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 12 points. Moss is 0 points ahead of Wolfgang von Trips.
