The 1994 Japanese Grand Prix started Nov 6, 1994 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Heinz-Harald Frentzen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 55 minutes and 53 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1994 Japanese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 92 points. Schumacher is 1 points ahead of Damon Hill.
