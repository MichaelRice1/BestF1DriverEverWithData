The 1994 Pacific Grand Prix started Apr 17, 1994 on Okayama International Circuit in Aida, Japan. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Damon Hill started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 01 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 1994 Pacific Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Schumacher is 13 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
