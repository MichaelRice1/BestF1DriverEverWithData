The 1994 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 14, 1994 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 48 minutes and 00 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Jos Verstappen in third place.

After the 1994 Hungarian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Schumacher is 31 points ahead of Damon Hill.
