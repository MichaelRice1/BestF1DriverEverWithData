The 2023 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 17, 2023 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from pole position with George Russell next to him. Charles Leclerc started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 37 seconds Carlos Sainz Jr. finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2023 Singapore Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 374 points. Verstappen is 151 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
