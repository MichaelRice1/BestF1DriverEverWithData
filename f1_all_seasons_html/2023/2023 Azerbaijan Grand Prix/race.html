The 2023 Azerbaijan Grand Prix started Apr 30, 2023 on Baku City Circuit in Baku, Azerbaijan. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 42 seconds Sergio Pérez finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2023 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 93 points. Verstappen is 6 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
