The 2023 Qatar Grand Prix started Oct 8, 2023 on Losail International Circuit in Lusail, Qatar. Max Verstappen started from pole position with George Russell next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 39 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Oscar Piastri in second and Lando Norris in third place.

After the 2023 Qatar Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 433 points. Verstappen is 209 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
