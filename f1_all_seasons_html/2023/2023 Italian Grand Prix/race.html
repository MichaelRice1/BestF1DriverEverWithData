The 2023 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 3, 2023 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Charles Leclerc started from third place.

In 1 hour, 13 minutes and 41 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2023 Italian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 364 points. Verstappen is 145 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
