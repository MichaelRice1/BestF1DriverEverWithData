The 2023 Las Vegas Grand Prix started Nov 18, 2023 on Las Vegas Street Circuit in Las Vegas, United States of America. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Carlos Sainz Jr. next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 08 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2023 Las Vegas Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 549 points. Verstappen is 276 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
