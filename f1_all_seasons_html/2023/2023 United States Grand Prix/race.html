The 2023 United States Grand Prix started Oct 22, 2023 on Circuit of the Americas in Austin, United States of America. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Lando Norris next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 21 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2023 United States Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 466 points. Verstappen is 226 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
