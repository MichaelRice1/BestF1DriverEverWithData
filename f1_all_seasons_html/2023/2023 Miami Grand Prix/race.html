The 2023 Miami Grand Prix started May 7, 2023 on Miami International Autodrome in Miami Gardens, United States of America. Sergio Pérez started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 38 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2023 Miami Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 119 points. Verstappen is 14 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
