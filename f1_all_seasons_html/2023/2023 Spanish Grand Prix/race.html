The 2023 Spanish Grand Prix started Jun 4, 2023 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Carlos Sainz Jr. next to him. Lando Norris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 57 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2023 Spanish Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 170 points. Verstappen is 53 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
