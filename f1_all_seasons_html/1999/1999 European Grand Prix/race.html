The 1999 European Grand Prix started Sep 26, 1999 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Heinz-Harald Frentzen started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 54 seconds Johnny Herbert finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jarno Trulli in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 1999 European Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 62 points. Häkkinen is 2 points ahead of Eddie Irvine.
