The 1999 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 13, 1999 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mika Häkkinen next to him. Eddie Irvine started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 35 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Giancarlo Fisichella in second and Eddie Irvine in third place.

After the 1999 Canadian Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 34 points. Häkkinen is 4 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
