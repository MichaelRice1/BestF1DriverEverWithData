The 1999 German Grand Prix started Aug 1, 1999 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Heinz-Harald Frentzen next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 21 minutes and 58 seconds Eddie Irvine finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mika Salo in second and Heinz-Harald Frentzen in third place.

After the 1999 German Grand Prix, Eddie Irvine is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 52 points. Irvine is 8 points ahead of Mika Häkkinen.
