The 1999 British Grand Prix started Jul 11, 1999 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 30 seconds David Coulthard finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Eddie Irvine in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 1999 British Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Häkkinen is 8 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
