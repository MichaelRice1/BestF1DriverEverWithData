The 1999 Brazilian Grand Prix started Apr 11, 1999 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 03 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Heinz-Harald Frentzen in third place.

After the 1999 Brazilian Grand Prix, Eddie Irvine is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 12 points. Irvine is 2 points ahead of Mika Häkkinen.
