The 1981 San Marino Grand Prix started May 3, 1981 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Gilles Villeneuve started from pole position with Carlos Reutemann next to him. René Arnoux started from third place.

In 1 hour, 51 minutes and 23 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Carlos Reutemann in third place.

After the 1981 San Marino Grand Prix, Carlos Reutemann is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 25 points. Reutemann is 3 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
