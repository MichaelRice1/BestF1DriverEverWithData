The 1981 United States Grand Prix West started Mar 15, 1981 on Long Beach in Long Beach, United States of America. Riccardo Patrese started from pole position with Alan Jones next to him. Carlos Reutemann started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 41 seconds Alan Jones finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Reutemann in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1981 United States Grand Prix West, Alan Jones is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Jones is 3 points ahead of Carlos Reutemann.
