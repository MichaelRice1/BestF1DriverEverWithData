The 1981 Caesars Palace Grand Prix started Oct 17, 1981 on Caesars Palace in Las Vegas, United States of America. Carlos Reutemann started from pole position with Alan Jones next to him. Gilles Villeneuve started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 09 seconds Alan Jones finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Bruno Giacomelli in third place.

After the 1981 Caesars Palace Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 50 points. Piquet is 1 points ahead of Carlos Reutemann.
