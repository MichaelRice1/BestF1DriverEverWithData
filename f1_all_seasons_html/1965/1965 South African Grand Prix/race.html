The 1965 South African Grand Prix started Jan 1, 1965 on Prince George Circuit in East London, South Africa. Jim Clark started from pole position with John Surtees next to him. Jack Brabham started from third place.

In 2 hour, 06 minutes and 46 seconds Jim Clark finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with John Surtees in second and Graham Hill in third place.

After the 1965 South African Grand Prix, Jim Clark is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Clark is 3 points ahead of John Surtees.
