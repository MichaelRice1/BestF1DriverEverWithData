The 1978 Austrian Grand Prix started Aug 13, 1978 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Ronnie Peterson started from pole position with Mario Andretti next to him. Jean-Pierre Jabouille started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 21 seconds Ronnie Peterson finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Depailler in second and Gilles Villeneuve in third place.

After the 1978 Austrian Grand Prix, Mario Andretti is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Andretti is 9 points ahead of Ronnie Peterson.
