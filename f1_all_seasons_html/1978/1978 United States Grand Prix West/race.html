The 1978 United States Grand Prix West started Apr 2, 1978 on Long Beach in Long Beach, United States of America. Carlos Reutemann started from pole position with Gilles Villeneuve next to him. Niki Lauda started from third place.

In 1 hour, 52 minutes and 01 seconds Carlos Reutemann finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mario Andretti in second and Patrick Depailler in third place.

After the 1978 United States Grand Prix West, Carlos Reutemann is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Reutemann is 0 points ahead of Mario Andretti.
