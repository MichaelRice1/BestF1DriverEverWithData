The 1968 Monaco Grand Prix started May 26, 1968 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Graham Hill started from pole position with Johnny Servoz-Gavin next to him. Jo Siffert started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 32 seconds Graham Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Richard Attwood in second and Lucien Bianchi in third place.

After the 1968 Monaco Grand Prix, Graham Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Hill is 14 points ahead of Denny Hulme.
