The 1968 French Grand Prix started Jul 7, 1968 on Rouen-Les-Essarts in Rouen, France. Jochen Rindt started from pole position with Jackie Stewart next to him. Jacky Ickx started from third place.

In 2 hour, 25 minutes and 40 seconds Jacky Ickx finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with John Surtees in second and Jackie Stewart in third place.

After the 1968 French Grand Prix, Graham Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Hill is 8 points ahead of Jackie Stewart.
