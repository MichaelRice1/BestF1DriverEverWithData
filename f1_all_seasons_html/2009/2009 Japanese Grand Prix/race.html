The 2009 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 4, 2009 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Jarno Trulli next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 20 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jarno Trulli in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2009 Japanese Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 85 points. Button is 14 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
