The 2009 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 1, 2009 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 03 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2009 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 95 points. Button is 11 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
