The 2009 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 27, 2009 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 56 minutes and 06 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Timo Glock in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2009 Singapore Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 84 points. Button is 15 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
