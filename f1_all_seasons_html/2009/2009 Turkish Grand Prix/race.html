The 2009 Turkish Grand Prix started Jun 7, 2009 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Jenson Button next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 24 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2009 Turkish Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 61 points. Button is 26 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
