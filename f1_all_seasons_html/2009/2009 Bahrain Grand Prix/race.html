The 2009 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 26, 2009 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Jarno Trulli started from pole position with Timo Glock next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 48 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Jarno Trulli in third place.

After the 2009 Bahrain Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 31 points. Button is 12 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
