The 2009 Brazilian Grand Prix started Oct 18, 2009 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Rubens Barrichello started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Adrian Sutil started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 23 seconds Mark Webber finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Robert Kubica in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2009 Brazilian Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 89 points. Button is 15 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
