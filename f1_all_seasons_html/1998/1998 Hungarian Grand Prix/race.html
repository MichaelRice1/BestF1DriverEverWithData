The 1998 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 16, 1998 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 45 minutes and 25 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Jacques Villeneuve in third place.

After the 1998 Hungarian Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 77 points. Häkkinen is 7 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
