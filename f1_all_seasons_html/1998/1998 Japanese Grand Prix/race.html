The 1998 Japanese Grand Prix started Nov 1, 1998 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mika Häkkinen next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 22 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Eddie Irvine in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 1998 Japanese Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 100 points. Häkkinen is 14 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
