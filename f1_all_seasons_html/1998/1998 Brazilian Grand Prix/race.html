The 1998 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 29, 1998 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Heinz-Harald Frentzen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 11 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1998 Brazilian Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Häkkinen is 8 points ahead of David Coulthard.
