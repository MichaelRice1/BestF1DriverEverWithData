The 1998 British Grand Prix started Jul 12, 1998 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Jacques Villeneuve started from third place.

In 1 hour, 47 minutes and 02 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mika Häkkinen in second and Eddie Irvine in third place.

After the 1998 British Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 56 points. Häkkinen is 2 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
