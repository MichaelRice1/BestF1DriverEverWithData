The 1989 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 23, 1989 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 51 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Alessandro Nannini in third place.

After the 1989 San Marino Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 12 points. Prost is 3 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
