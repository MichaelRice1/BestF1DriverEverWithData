The 1989 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 24, 1989 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 48 seconds Gerhard Berger finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Stefan Johansson in third place.

After the 1989 Portuguese Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 75 points. Prost is 24 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
