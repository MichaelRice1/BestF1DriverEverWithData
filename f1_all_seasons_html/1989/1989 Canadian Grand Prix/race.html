The 1989 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 18, 1989 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Alain Prost started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Riccardo Patrese started from third place.

In 2 hour, 01 minutes and 24 seconds Thierry Boutsen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Andrea de Cesaris in third place.

After the 1989 Canadian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 29 points. Prost is 2 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
