The 1989 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 5, 1989 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Pierluigi Martini started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 17 seconds Thierry Boutsen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alessandro Nannini in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1989 Australian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Prost is 16 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
