The 2016 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 3, 2016 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 34 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2016 Bahrain Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 50 points. Rosberg is 17 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
