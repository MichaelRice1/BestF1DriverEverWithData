The 2016 Brazilian Grand Prix started Nov 13, 2016 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 3 hour, 01 minutes and 01 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2016 Brazilian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 367 points. Rosberg is 12 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
