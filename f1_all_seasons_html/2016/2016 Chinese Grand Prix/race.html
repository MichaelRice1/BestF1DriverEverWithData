The 2016 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 17, 2016 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Daniel Ricciardo next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 53 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Daniil Kvyat in third place.

After the 2016 Chinese Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 75 points. Rosberg is 36 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
