The 2016 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 9, 2016 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 43 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2016 Japanese Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 313 points. Rosberg is 33 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
