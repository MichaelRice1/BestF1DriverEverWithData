The 2016 German Grand Prix started Jul 31, 2016 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 44 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Daniel Ricciardo in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2016 German Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 217 points. Hamilton is 19 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
