The 2014 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 6, 2014 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 42 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2014 Bahrain Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 61 points. Rosberg is 11 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
