The 2014 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 27, 2014 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 53 minutes and 05 seconds Daniel Ricciardo finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2014 Hungarian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 202 points. Rosberg is 11 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
