The 2014 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 30, 2014 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 25 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2014 Malaysian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 43 points. Rosberg is 18 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
