The 2014 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 23, 2014 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 02 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Felipe Massa in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2014 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 384 points. Hamilton is 67 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
