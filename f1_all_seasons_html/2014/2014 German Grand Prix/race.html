The 2014 German Grand Prix started Jul 20, 2014 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 42 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2014 German Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 190 points. Rosberg is 14 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
