The 2014 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 20, 2014 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Daniel Ricciardo next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 28 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2014 Chinese Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 79 points. Rosberg is 4 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
