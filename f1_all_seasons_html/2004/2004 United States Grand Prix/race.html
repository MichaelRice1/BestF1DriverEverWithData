The 2004 United States Grand Prix started Jun 20, 2004 on Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Indianapolis, United States of America. Rubens Barrichello started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Takuma Sato started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 29 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Takuma Sato in third place.

After the 2004 United States Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 80 points. Schumacher is 18 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
