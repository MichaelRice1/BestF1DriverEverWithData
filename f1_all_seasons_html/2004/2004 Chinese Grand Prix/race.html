The 2004 Chinese Grand Prix started Sep 26, 2004 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Rubens Barrichello started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 12 seconds Rubens Barrichello finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2004 Chinese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 136 points. Schumacher is 28 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
