The 2004 European Grand Prix started May 30, 2004 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Takuma Sato next to him. Jarno Trulli started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 35 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2004 European Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 60 points. Schumacher is 14 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
