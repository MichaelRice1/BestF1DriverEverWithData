The 2004 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 4, 2004 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Juan Pablo Montoya started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 34 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2004 Bahrain Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Schumacher is 9 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
