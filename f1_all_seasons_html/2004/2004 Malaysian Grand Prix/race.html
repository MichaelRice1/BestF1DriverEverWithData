The 2004 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 21, 2004 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 07 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2004 Malaysian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Schumacher is 7 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
