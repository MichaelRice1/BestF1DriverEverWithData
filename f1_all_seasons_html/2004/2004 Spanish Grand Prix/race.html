The 2004 Spanish Grand Prix started May 9, 2004 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Takuma Sato started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 32 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Jarno Trulli in third place.

After the 2004 Spanish Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 50 points. Schumacher is 18 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
