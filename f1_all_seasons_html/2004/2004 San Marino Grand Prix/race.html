The 2004 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 25, 2004 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Jenson Button started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Juan Pablo Montoya started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 19 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Juan Pablo Montoya in third place.

After the 2004 San Marino Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Schumacher is 16 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
