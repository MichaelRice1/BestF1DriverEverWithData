The 2004 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 7, 2004 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Juan Pablo Montoya started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 15 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2004 Australian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Schumacher is 2 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
