The 1996 San Marino Grand Prix started May 5, 1996 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Jacques Villeneuve started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 26 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1996 San Marino Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 43 points. Hill is 21 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
