The 1996 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 22, 1996 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Damon Hill started from pole position with Jacques Villeneuve next to him. Jean Alesi started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 22 seconds Jacques Villeneuve finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1996 Portuguese Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 87 points. Hill is 9 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
