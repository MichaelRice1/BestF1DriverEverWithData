The 1996 French Grand Prix started Jun 30, 1996 on Circuit de Nevers Magny-Cours in Nevers, France. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Jean Alesi started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 28 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacques Villeneuve in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1996 French Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 63 points. Hill is 25 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
