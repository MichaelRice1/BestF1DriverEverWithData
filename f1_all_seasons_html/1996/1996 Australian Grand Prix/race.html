The 1996 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 10, 1996 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Jacques Villeneuve started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Eddie Irvine started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 50 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacques Villeneuve in second and Eddie Irvine in third place.

After the 1996 Australian Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Hill is 4 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
