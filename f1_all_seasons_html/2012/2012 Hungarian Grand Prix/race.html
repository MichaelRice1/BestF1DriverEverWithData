The 2012 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 29, 2012 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Romain Grosjean next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 05 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Romain Grosjean in third place.

After the 2012 Hungarian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 164 points. Alonso is 40 points ahead of Mark Webber.
