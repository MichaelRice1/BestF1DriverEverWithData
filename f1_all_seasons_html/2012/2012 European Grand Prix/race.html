The 2012 European Grand Prix started Jun 24, 2012 on Valencia Street Circuit in Valencia, Spain. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Pastor Maldonado started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 16 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 2012 European Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 111 points. Alonso is 20 points ahead of Mark Webber.
