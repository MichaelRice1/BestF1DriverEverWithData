The 2012 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 4, 2012 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Pastor Maldonado started from third place.

In 1 hour, 45 minutes and 58 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2012 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 255 points. Vettel is 10 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
