The 2012 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 10, 2012 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 29 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Romain Grosjean in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2012 Canadian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 88 points. Hamilton is 2 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
