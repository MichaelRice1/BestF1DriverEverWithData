The 2012 Monaco Grand Prix started May 27, 2012 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 06 seconds Mark Webber finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2012 Monaco Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Alonso is 3 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
