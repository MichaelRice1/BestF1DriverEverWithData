The 2012 United States Grand Prix started Nov 18, 2012 on Circuit of the Americas in Austin, United States of America. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 55 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2012 United States Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 273 points. Vettel is 13 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
