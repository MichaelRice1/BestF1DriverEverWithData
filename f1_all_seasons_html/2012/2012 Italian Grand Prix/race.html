The 2012 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 9, 2012 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Jenson Button next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 1 hour, 19 minutes and 41 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2012 Italian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 179 points. Alonso is 37 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
