The 2020 Eifel Grand Prix started Oct 11, 2020 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 49 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2020 Eifel Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 230 points. Hamilton is 69 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
