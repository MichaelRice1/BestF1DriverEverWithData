The 2020 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 6, 2020 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 47 minutes and 06 seconds Pierre Gasly finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Sainz Jr. in second and Lance Stroll in third place.

After the 2020 Italian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 164 points. Hamilton is 47 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
