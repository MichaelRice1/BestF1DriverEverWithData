The 2020 Russian Grand Prix started Sep 27, 2020 on Sochi Autodrom in Sochi, Russia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 00 seconds Valtteri Bottas finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2020 Russian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 205 points. Hamilton is 44 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
