The 1973 Argentine Grand Prix started Jan 28, 1973 on Autódromo Juan y Oscar Gálvez in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Clay Regazzoni started from pole position with Emerson Fittipaldi next to him. Jacky Ickx started from third place.

In 1 hour, 56 minutes and 18 seconds Emerson Fittipaldi finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with François Cevert in second and Jackie Stewart in third place.

After the 1973 Argentine Grand Prix, Emerson Fittipaldi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Fittipaldi is 3 points ahead of François Cevert.
