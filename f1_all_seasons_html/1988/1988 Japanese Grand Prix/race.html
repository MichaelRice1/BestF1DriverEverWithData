The 1988 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 30, 1988 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 26 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Thierry Boutsen in third place.

After the 1988 Japanese Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 87 points. Senna is 3 points ahead of Alain Prost.
