The 1988 British Grand Prix started Jul 10, 1988 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Michele Alboreto next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 16 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Alessandro Nannini in third place.

After the 1988 British Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Prost is 6 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
