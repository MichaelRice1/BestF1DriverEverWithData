The 1988 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 28, 1988 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 00 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Ivan Capelli in third place.

After the 1988 Belgian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 75 points. Senna is 3 points ahead of Alain Prost.
