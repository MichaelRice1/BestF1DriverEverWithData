The 1988 San Marino Grand Prix started May 1, 1988 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 41 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1988 San Marino Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Prost is 6 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
