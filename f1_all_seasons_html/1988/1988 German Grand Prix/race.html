The 1988 German Grand Prix started Jul 24, 1988 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 54 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1988 German Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 60 points. Prost is 3 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
