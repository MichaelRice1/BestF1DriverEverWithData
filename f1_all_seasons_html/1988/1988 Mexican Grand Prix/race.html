The 1988 Mexican Grand Prix started May 29, 1988 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 15 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1988 Mexican Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 33 points. Prost is 15 points ahead of Gerhard Berger.
