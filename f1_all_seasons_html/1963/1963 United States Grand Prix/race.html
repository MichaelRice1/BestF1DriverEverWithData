The 1963 United States Grand Prix started Oct 6, 1963 on Watkins Glen International in Watkins Glen, United States of America. Graham Hill started from pole position with Jim Clark next to him. John Surtees started from third place.

In 2 hour, 19 minutes and 22 seconds Graham Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Richie Ginther in second and Jim Clark in third place.

After the 1963 United States Grand Prix, Jim Clark is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 51 points. Clark is 23 points ahead of Richie Ginther.
