The 1963 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 8, 1963 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. John Surtees started from pole position with Graham Hill next to him. Jim Clark started from third place.

In 2 hour, 24 minutes and 19 seconds Jim Clark finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Richie Ginther in second and Bruce McLaren in third place.

After the 1963 Italian Grand Prix, Jim Clark is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 51 points. Clark is 27 points ahead of Richie Ginther.
