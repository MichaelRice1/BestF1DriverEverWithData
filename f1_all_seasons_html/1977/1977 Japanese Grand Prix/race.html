The 1977 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 23, 1977 on Fuji Speedway in Oyama, Japan. Mario Andretti started from pole position with James Hunt next to him. John Watson started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 51 seconds James Hunt finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Reutemann in second and Patrick Depailler in third place.

After the 1977 Japanese Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Lauda is 17 points ahead of Jody Scheckter.
