The 1977 Argentine Grand Prix started Jan 9, 1977 on Autódromo Juan y Oscar Gálvez in Buenos Aires, Argentina. James Hunt started from pole position with John Watson next to him. Patrick Depailler started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 11 seconds Jody Scheckter finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Pace in second and Carlos Reutemann in third place.

After the 1977 Argentine Grand Prix, Jody Scheckter is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Scheckter is 3 points ahead of Carlos Pace.
