The 1977 United States Grand Prix West started Apr 3, 1977 on Long Beach in Long Beach, United States of America. Niki Lauda started from pole position with Mario Andretti next to him. Jody Scheckter started from third place.

In 1 hour, 51 minutes and 35 seconds Mario Andretti finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and Jody Scheckter in third place.

After the 1977 United States Grand Prix West, Jody Scheckter is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 19 points. Scheckter is 0 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
