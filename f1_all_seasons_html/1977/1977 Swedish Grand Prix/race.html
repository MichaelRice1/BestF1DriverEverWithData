The 1977 Swedish Grand Prix started Jun 19, 1977 on Anderstorp Raceway in Anderstorp, Sweden. Mario Andretti started from pole position with John Watson next to him. James Hunt started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 55 seconds Jacques Laffite finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jochen Mass in second and Carlos Reutemann in third place.

After the 1977 Swedish Grand Prix, Jody Scheckter is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 32 points. Scheckter is 1 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
