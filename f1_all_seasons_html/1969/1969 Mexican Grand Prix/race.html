The 1969 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 19, 1969 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Jack Brabham started from pole position with Jacky Ickx next to him. Jackie Stewart started from third place.

In 1 hour, 54 minutes and 08 seconds Denny Hulme finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacky Ickx in second and Jack Brabham in third place.

After the 1969 Mexican Grand Prix, Jackie Stewart is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 63 points. Stewart is 26 points ahead of Jacky Ickx.
