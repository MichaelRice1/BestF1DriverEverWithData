The 1992 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 16, 1992 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Riccardo Patrese started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 19 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1992 Hungarian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 92 points. Mansell is 52 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
