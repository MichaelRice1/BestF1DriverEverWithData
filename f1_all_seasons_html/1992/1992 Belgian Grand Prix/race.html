The 1992 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 30, 1992 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 10 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1992 Belgian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 98 points. Mansell is 54 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
