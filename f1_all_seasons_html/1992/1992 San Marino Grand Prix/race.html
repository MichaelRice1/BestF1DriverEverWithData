The 1992 San Marino Grand Prix started May 17, 1992 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 40 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1992 San Marino Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 50 points. Mansell is 26 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
