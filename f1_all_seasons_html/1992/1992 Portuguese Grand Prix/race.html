The 1992 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 27, 1992 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 46 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1992 Portuguese Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 108 points. Mansell is 58 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
