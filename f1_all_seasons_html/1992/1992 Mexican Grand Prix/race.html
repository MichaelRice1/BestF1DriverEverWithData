The 1992 Mexican Grand Prix started Mar 22, 1992 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 53 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1992 Mexican Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Mansell is 8 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
