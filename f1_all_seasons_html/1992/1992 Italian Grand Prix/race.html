The 1992 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 13, 1992 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Jean Alesi started from third place.

In 1 hour, 18 minutes and 15 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Martin Brundle in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1992 Italian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 98 points. Mansell is 51 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
