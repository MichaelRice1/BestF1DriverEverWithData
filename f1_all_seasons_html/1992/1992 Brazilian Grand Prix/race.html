The 1992 Brazilian Grand Prix started Apr 5, 1992 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 51 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1992 Brazilian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Mansell is 12 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
