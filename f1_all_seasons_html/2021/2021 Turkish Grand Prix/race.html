The 2021 Turkish Grand Prix started Oct 3, 2021 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 04 seconds Valtteri Bottas finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2021 Turkish Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 262.5 points. Verstappen is 6 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
