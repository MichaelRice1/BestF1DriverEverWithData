The 2021 Qatar Grand Prix started Nov 21, 2021 on Losail International Circuit in Lusail, Qatar. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 28 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2021 Qatar Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 351.5 points. Verstappen is 8 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
