The 2021 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 1, 2021 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 2 hour, 04 minutes and 43 seconds Esteban Ocon finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2021 Hungarian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 195 points. Hamilton is 8 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
