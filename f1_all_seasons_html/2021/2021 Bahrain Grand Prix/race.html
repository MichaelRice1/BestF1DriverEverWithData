The 2021 Bahrain Grand Prix started Mar 28, 2021 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 03 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2021 Bahrain Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 25 points. Hamilton is 7 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
