The 2021 Styrian Grand Prix started Jun 27, 2021 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 22 minutes and 18 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2021 Styrian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 156 points. Verstappen is 18 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
