The 2021 São Paulo Grand Prix started Nov 14, 2021 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 22 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2021 São Paulo Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 332.5 points. Verstappen is 14 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
