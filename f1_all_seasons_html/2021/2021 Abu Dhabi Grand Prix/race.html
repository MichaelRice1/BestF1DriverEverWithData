The 2021 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Dec 12, 2021 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Lando Norris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 17 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2021 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 395.5 points. Verstappen is 8 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
