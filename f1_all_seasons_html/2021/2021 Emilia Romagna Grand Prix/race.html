The 2021 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix started Apr 18, 2021 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 2 hour, 02 minutes and 34 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Lando Norris in third place.

After the 2021 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 44 points. Hamilton is 1 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
