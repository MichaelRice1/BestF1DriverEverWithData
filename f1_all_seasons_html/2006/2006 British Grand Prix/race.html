The 2006 British Grand Prix started Jun 11, 2006 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 25 minutes and 51 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2006 British Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 74 points. Alonso is 23 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
