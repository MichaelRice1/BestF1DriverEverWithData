The 2006 Monaco Grand Prix started May 28, 2006 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 43 minutes and 43 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2006 Monaco Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 64 points. Alonso is 21 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
