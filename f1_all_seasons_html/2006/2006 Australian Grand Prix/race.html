The 2006 Australian Grand Prix started Apr 2, 2006 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Jenson Button started from pole position with Giancarlo Fisichella next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 27 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 2006 Australian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 28 points. Alonso is 14 points ahead of Giancarlo Fisichella.
