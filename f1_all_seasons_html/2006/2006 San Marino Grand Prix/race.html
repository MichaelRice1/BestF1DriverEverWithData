The 2006 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 23, 2006 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Jenson Button next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 06 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Juan Pablo Montoya in third place.

After the 2006 San Marino Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 36 points. Alonso is 15 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
