The 2006 Bahrain Grand Prix started Mar 12, 2006 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Felipe Massa next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 46 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2006 Bahrain Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Alonso is 2 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
