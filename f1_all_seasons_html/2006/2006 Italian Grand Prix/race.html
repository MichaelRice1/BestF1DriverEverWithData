The 2006 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 10, 2006 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Nick Heidfeld started from third place.

In 1 hour, 14 minutes and 51 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Robert Kubica in third place.

After the 2006 Italian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 108 points. Alonso is 2 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
