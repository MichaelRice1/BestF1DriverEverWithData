The 2006 Brazilian Grand Prix started Oct 22, 2006 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Jarno Trulli started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 53 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2006 Brazilian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 134 points. Alonso is 13 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
