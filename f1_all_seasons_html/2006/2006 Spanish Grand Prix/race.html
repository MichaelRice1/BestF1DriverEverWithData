The 2006 Spanish Grand Prix started May 14, 2006 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Giancarlo Fisichella next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 21 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Giancarlo Fisichella in third place.

After the 2006 Spanish Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Alonso is 15 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
