The 2006 Turkish Grand Prix started Aug 27, 2006 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 51 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 2006 Turkish Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 108 points. Alonso is 12 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
