The 2006 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 19, 2006 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Giancarlo Fisichella started from pole position with Jenson Button next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 40 seconds Giancarlo Fisichella finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2006 Malaysian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Alonso is 7 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
