The 1984 European Grand Prix started Oct 7, 1984 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Patrick Tambay started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 13 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michele Alboreto in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1984 European Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 66 points. Lauda is 3.5 points ahead of Alain Prost.
