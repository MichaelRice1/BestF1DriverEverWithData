The 1984 Detroit Grand Prix started Jun 24, 1984 on Detroit Street Circuit in Detroit, United States of America. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 55 minutes and 41 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Elio de Angelis in second and Teo Fabi in third place.

After the 1984 Detroit Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 35.5 points. Prost is 11.5 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
