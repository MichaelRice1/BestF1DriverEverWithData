The 1984 Portuguese Grand Prix started Oct 21, 1984 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 11 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1984 Portuguese Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Lauda is 0.5 points ahead of Alain Prost.
