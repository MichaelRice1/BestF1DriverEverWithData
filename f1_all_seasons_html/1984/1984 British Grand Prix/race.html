The 1984 British Grand Prix started Jul 22, 1984 on Brands Hatch in Fawkham, United Kingdom. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Niki Lauda started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 28 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Derek Warwick in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1984 British Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 35.5 points. Prost is 2.5 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
