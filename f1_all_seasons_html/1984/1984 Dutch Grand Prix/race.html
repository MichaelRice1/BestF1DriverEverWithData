The 1984 Dutch Grand Prix started Aug 26, 1984 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Alain Prost started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Elio de Angelis started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 21 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and Nigel Mansell in third place.

After the 1984 Dutch Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Lauda is 0.5 points ahead of Alain Prost.
