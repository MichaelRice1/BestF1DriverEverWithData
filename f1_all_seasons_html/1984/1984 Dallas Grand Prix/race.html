The 1984 Dallas Grand Prix started Jul 8, 1984 on Fair Park in Dallas, United States of America. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Elio de Angelis next to him. Derek Warwick started from third place.

In 2 hour, 01 minutes and 22 seconds Keke Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with René Arnoux in second and Elio de Angelis in third place.

After the 1984 Dallas Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 35.5 points. Prost is 9.5 points ahead of Elio de Angelis.
