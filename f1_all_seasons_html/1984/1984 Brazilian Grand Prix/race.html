The 1984 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 25, 1984 on Autódromo Internacional Nelson Piquet in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Elio de Angelis started from pole position with Michele Alboreto next to him. Derek Warwick started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 34 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Keke Rosberg in second and Elio de Angelis in third place.

After the 1984 Brazilian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Prost is 3 points ahead of Keke Rosberg.
