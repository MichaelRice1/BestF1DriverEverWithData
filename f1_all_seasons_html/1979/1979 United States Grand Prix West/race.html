The 1979 United States Grand Prix West started Apr 8, 1979 on Long Beach in Long Beach, United States of America. Gilles Villeneuve started from pole position with Carlos Reutemann next to him. Jody Scheckter started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 25 seconds Gilles Villeneuve finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jody Scheckter in second and Alan Jones in third place.

After the 1979 United States Grand Prix West, Gilles Villeneuve is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Villeneuve is 2 points ahead of Jacques Laffite.
