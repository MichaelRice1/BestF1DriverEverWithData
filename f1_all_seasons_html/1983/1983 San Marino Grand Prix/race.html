The 1983 San Marino Grand Prix started May 1, 1983 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. René Arnoux started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Patrick Tambay started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 52 seconds Patrick Tambay finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and René Arnoux in third place.

After the 1983 San Marino Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Piquet is 0 points ahead of Alain Prost.
