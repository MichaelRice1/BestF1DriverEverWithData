The 1983 Dutch Grand Prix started Aug 28, 1983 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Patrick Tambay next to him. Elio de Angelis started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 41 seconds René Arnoux finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Tambay in second and John Watson in third place.

After the 1983 Dutch Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 51 points. Prost is 8 points ahead of René Arnoux.
