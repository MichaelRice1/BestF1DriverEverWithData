The 1983 United States Grand Prix West started Mar 27, 1983 on Long Beach in Long Beach, United States of America. Patrick Tambay started from pole position with René Arnoux next to him. Keke Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 53 minutes and 34 seconds John Watson finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and René Arnoux in third place.

After the 1983 United States Grand Prix West, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Lauda is 1 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
