The 1990 Mexican Grand Prix started Jun 24, 1990 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 35 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1990 Mexican Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 31 points. Senna is 8 points ahead of Alain Prost.
