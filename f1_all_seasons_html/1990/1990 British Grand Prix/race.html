The 1990 British Grand Prix started Jul 15, 1990 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 18 minutes and 30 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Thierry Boutsen in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1990 British Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 41 points. Prost is 2 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
