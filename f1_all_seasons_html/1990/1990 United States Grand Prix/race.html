The 1990 United States Grand Prix started Mar 11, 1990 on Phoenix Street Circuit in Phoenix, United States of America. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Pierluigi Martini next to him. Andrea de Cesaris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 52 minutes and 32 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and Thierry Boutsen in third place.

After the 1990 United States Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Senna is 3 points ahead of Jean Alesi.
