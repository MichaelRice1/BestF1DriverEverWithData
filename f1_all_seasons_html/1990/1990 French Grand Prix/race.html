The 1990 French Grand Prix started Jul 8, 1990 on Circuit Paul Ricard in Le Castellet, France. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 29 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ivan Capelli in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1990 French Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 35 points. Senna is 3 points ahead of Alain Prost.
