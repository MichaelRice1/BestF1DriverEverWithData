The 1990 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 4, 1990 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 44 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1990 Australian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 78 points. Senna is 7 points ahead of Alain Prost.
