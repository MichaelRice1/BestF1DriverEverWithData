The 1990 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 12, 1990 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Thierry Boutsen started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 30 seconds Thierry Boutsen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1990 Hungarian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Senna is 10 points ahead of Alain Prost.
