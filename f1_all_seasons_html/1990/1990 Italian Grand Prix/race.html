The 1990 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 9, 1990 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 17 minutes and 57 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1990 Italian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Senna is 16 points ahead of Alain Prost.
