The 2010 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 26, 2010 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 57 minutes and 53 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2010 Singapore Grand Prix, Mark Webber is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 202 points. Webber is 11 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
