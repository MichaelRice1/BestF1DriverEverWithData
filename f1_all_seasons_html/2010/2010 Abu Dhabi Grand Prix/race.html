The 2010 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 14, 2010 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 36 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2010 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 256 points. Vettel is 4 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
