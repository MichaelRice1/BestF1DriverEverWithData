The 2010 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 10, 2010 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 27 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2010 Japanese Grand Prix, Mark Webber is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 220 points. Webber is 14 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
