The 2010 Korean Grand Prix started Oct 24, 2010 on Korea International Circuit in Yeongam, South Korea. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 2 hour, 48 minutes and 20 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2010 Korean Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 231 points. Alonso is 11 points ahead of Mark Webber.
