The 2010 Malaysian Grand Prix started Apr 4, 2010 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Mark Webber started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 48 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Nico Rosberg in third place.

After the 2010 Malaysian Grand Prix, Felipe Massa is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 39 points. Massa is 2 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
