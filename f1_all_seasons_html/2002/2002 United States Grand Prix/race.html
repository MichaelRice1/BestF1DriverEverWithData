The 2002 United States Grand Prix started Sep 29, 2002 on Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Indianapolis, United States of America. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 07 seconds Rubens Barrichello finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2002 United States Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 134 points. Schumacher is 63 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
