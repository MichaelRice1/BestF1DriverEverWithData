The 2002 European Grand Prix started Jun 23, 2002 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Juan Pablo Montoya started from pole position with Ralf Schumacher next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 07 seconds Rubens Barrichello finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2002 European Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Schumacher is 46 points ahead of Ralf Schumacher.
