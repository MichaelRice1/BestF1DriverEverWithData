The 2002 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 17, 2002 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 12 seconds Ralf Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 2002 Malaysian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 14 points. Schumacher is 2 points ahead of Juan Pablo Montoya.
