The 2005 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 3, 2005 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Jarno Trulli started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 18 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jarno Trulli in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2005 Bahrain Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 26 points. Alonso is 10 points ahead of Jarno Trulli.
