The 2005 Spanish Grand Prix started May 8, 2005 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 16 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Jarno Trulli in third place.

After the 2005 Spanish Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 44 points. Alonso is 18 points ahead of Jarno Trulli.
