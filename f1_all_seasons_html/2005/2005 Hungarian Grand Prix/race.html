The 2005 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 31, 2005 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Jarno Trulli started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 25 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 2005 Hungarian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 87 points. Alonso is 26 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
