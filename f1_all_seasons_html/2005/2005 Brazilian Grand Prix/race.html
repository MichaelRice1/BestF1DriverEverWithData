The 2005 Brazilian Grand Prix started Sep 25, 2005 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Giancarlo Fisichella started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 20 seconds Juan Pablo Montoya finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2005 Brazilian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 117 points. Alonso is 23 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
