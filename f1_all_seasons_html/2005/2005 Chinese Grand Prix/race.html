The 2005 Chinese Grand Prix started Oct 16, 2005 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Giancarlo Fisichella next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 53 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 2005 Chinese Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 133 points. Alonso is 21 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
