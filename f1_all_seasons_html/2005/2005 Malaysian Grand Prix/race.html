The 2005 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 20, 2005 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Jarno Trulli next to him. Giancarlo Fisichella started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 33 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jarno Trulli in second and Nick Heidfeld in third place.

After the 2005 Malaysian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 16 points. Alonso is 6 points ahead of Giancarlo Fisichella.
