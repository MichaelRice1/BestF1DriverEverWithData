The 2005 United States Grand Prix started Jun 19, 2005 on Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Indianapolis, United States of America. Jarno Trulli started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 43 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Tiago Monteiro in third place.

After the 2005 United States Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 59 points. Alonso is 22 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
