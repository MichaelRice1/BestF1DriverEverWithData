The 1960 British Grand Prix started Jul 16, 1960 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Jack Brabham started from pole position with Graham Hill next to him. Bruce McLaren started from third place.

In 2 hour, 04 minutes and 24 seconds Jack Brabham finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with John Surtees in second and Innes Ireland in third place.

After the 1960 British Grand Prix, Jack Brabham is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 32 points. Brabham is 5 points ahead of Bruce McLaren.
