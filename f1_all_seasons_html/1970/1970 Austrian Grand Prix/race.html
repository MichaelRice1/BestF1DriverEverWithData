The 1970 Austrian Grand Prix started Aug 16, 1970 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Jochen Rindt started from pole position with Clay Regazzoni next to him. Jacky Ickx started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 17 seconds Jacky Ickx finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Clay Regazzoni in second and Rolf Stommelen in third place.

After the 1970 Austrian Grand Prix, Jochen Rindt is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 45 points. Rindt is 20 points ahead of Jack Brabham.
