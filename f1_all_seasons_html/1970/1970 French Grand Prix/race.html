The 1970 French Grand Prix started Jul 5, 1970 on Charade Circuit in Clermont-Ferrand, France. Jacky Ickx started from pole position with Jean-Pierre Beltoise next to him. Chris Amon started from third place.

In 1 hour, 55 minutes and 57 seconds Jochen Rindt finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Chris Amon in second and Jack Brabham in third place.

After the 1970 French Grand Prix, Jochen Rindt is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 27 points. Rindt is 8 points ahead of Jackie Stewart.
