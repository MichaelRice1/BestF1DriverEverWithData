The 1970 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 25, 1970 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Clay Regazzoni started from pole position with Jackie Stewart next to him. Jacky Ickx started from third place.

In 1 hour, 53 minutes and 28 seconds Jacky Ickx finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Clay Regazzoni in second and Denny Hulme in third place.

After the 1970 Mexican Grand Prix, Jochen Rindt is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 45 points. Rindt is 5 points ahead of Jacky Ickx.
