The 1980 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 14, 1980 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. René Arnoux started from pole position with Jean-Pierre Jabouille next to him. Carlos Reutemann started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 07 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alan Jones in second and Carlos Reutemann in third place.

After the 1980 Italian Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Piquet is 1 points ahead of Alan Jones.
