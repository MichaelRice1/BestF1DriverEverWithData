The 1997 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 9, 1997 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Jacques Villeneuve started from pole position with Heinz-Harald Frentzen next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 28 seconds David Coulthard finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Mika Häkkinen in third place.

After the 1997 Australian Grand Prix, David Coulthard is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Coulthard is 4 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
