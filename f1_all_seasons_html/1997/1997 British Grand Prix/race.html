The 1997 British Grand Prix started Jul 13, 1997 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Jacques Villeneuve started from pole position with Heinz-Harald Frentzen next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 01 seconds Jacques Villeneuve finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and Alexander Wurz in third place.

After the 1997 British Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 47 points. Schumacher is 4 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
