The 1997 Argentine Grand Prix started Apr 13, 1997 on Autódromo Juan y Oscar Gálvez in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Jacques Villeneuve started from pole position with Heinz-Harald Frentzen next to him. Olivier Panis started from third place.

In 1 hour, 52 minutes and 01 seconds Jacques Villeneuve finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Eddie Irvine in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 1997 Argentine Grand Prix, Jacques Villeneuve is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Villeneuve is 10 points ahead of David Coulthard.
