The 1997 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 10, 1997 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Jacques Villeneuve next to him. Damon Hill started from third place.

In 1 hour, 45 minutes and 47 seconds Jacques Villeneuve finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Johnny Herbert in third place.

After the 1997 Hungarian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 56 points. Schumacher is 3 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
