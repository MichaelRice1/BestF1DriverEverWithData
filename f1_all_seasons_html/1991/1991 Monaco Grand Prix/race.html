The 1991 Monaco Grand Prix started May 12, 1991 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Stefano Modena next to him. Riccardo Patrese started from third place.

In 1 hour, 53 minutes and 02 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1991 Monaco Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Senna is 29 points ahead of Alain Prost.
