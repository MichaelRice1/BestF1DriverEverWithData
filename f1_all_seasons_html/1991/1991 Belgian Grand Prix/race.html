The 1991 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 25, 1991 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 17 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1991 Belgian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 71 points. Senna is 22 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
