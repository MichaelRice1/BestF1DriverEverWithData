The 1991 Mexican Grand Prix started Jun 16, 1991 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Riccardo Patrese started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 52 seconds Riccardo Patrese finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1991 Mexican Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 44 points. Senna is 24 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
