The 1991 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 22, 1991 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Riccardo Patrese started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 42 seconds Riccardo Patrese finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1991 Portuguese Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 83 points. Senna is 24 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
