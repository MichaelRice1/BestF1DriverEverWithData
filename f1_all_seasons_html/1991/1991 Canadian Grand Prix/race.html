The 1991 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 2, 1991 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Riccardo Patrese started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 51 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Stefano Modena in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1991 Canadian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Senna is 24 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
