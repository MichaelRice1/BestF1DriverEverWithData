The 1985 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 3, 1985 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Keke Rosberg started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 40 seconds Keke Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacques Laffite in second and Philippe Streiff in third place.

After the 1985 Australian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 73 points. Prost is 20 points ahead of Michele Alboreto.
