The 1985 South African Grand Prix started Oct 19, 1985 on Kyalami Racing Circuit in Midrand, South Africa. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Keke Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 22 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Keke Rosberg in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1985 South African Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 73 points. Prost is 20 points ahead of Michele Alboreto.
