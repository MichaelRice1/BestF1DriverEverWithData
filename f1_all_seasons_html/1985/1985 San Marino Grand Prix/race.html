The 1985 San Marino Grand Prix started May 5, 1985 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Keke Rosberg next to him. Elio de Angelis started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 35 seconds Elio de Angelis finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Thierry Boutsen in second and Patrick Tambay in third place.

After the 1985 San Marino Grand Prix, Elio de Angelis is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 16 points. De Angelis is 4 points ahead of Michele Alboreto.
