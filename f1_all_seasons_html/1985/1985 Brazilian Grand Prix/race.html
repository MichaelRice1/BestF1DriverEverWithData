The 1985 Brazilian Grand Prix started Apr 7, 1985 on Autódromo Internacional Nelson Piquet in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Michele Alboreto started from pole position with Keke Rosberg next to him. Elio de Angelis started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 26 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michele Alboreto in second and Elio de Angelis in third place.

After the 1985 Brazilian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Prost is 3 points ahead of Michele Alboreto.
