The 1985 European Grand Prix started Oct 6, 1985 on Brands Hatch in Fawkham, United Kingdom. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 58 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Keke Rosberg in third place.

After the 1985 European Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Prost is 19 points ahead of Michele Alboreto.
