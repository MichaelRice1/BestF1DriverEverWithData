The 1985 Belgian Grand Prix started Sep 15, 1985 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Alain Prost started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 19 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1985 Belgian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 69 points. Prost is 16 points ahead of Michele Alboreto.
