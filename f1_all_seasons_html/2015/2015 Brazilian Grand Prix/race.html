The 2015 Brazilian Grand Prix started Nov 15, 2015 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 09 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2015 Brazilian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 363 points. Hamilton is 66 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
