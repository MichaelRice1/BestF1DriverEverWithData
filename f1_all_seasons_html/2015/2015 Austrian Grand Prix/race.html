The 2015 Austrian Grand Prix started Jun 21, 2015 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 16 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2015 Austrian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 169 points. Hamilton is 10 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
