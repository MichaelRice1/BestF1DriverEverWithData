The 2015 United States Grand Prix started Oct 25, 2015 on Circuit of the Americas in Austin, United States of America. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 52 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2015 United States Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 327 points. Hamilton is 76 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
