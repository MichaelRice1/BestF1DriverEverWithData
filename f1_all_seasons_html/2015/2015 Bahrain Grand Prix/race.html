The 2015 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 19, 2015 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 05 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Nico Rosberg in third place.

After the 2015 Bahrain Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 93 points. Hamilton is 27 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
