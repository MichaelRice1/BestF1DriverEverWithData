The 2015 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 26, 2015 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 09 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Daniil Kvyat in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2015 Hungarian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 202 points. Hamilton is 21 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
