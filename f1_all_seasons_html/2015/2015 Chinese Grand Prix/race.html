The 2015 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 12, 2015 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 42 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2015 Chinese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 68 points. Hamilton is 13 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
