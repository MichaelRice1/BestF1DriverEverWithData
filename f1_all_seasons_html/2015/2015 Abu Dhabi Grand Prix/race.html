The 2015 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 29, 2015 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 30 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2015 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 381 points. Hamilton is 59 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
