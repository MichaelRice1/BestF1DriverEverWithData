The 2015 Spanish Grand Prix started May 10, 2015 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 12 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2015 Spanish Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 111 points. Hamilton is 20 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
