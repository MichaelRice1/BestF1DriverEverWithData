The 1950 Swiss Grand Prix started Jun 4, 1950 on Circuit Bremgarten in Bern, Switzerland. Juan Manuel Fangio started from pole position with Nino Farina next to him. Luigi Fagioli started from third place.

In 2 hour, 02 minutes and 53 seconds Nino Farina finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Luigi Fagioli in second and Louis Rosier in third place.

After the 1950 Swiss Grand Prix, Nino Farina is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Farina is 6 points ahead of Luigi Fagioli.
