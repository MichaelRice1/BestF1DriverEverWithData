The 1950 Belgian Grand Prix started Jun 18, 1950 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Nino Farina started from pole position with Juan Manuel Fangio next to him. Luigi Fagioli started from third place.

In 2 hour, 47 minutes and 26 seconds Juan Manuel Fangio finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Luigi Fagioli in second and Louis Rosier in third place.

After the 1950 Belgian Grand Prix, Nino Farina is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 22 points. Farina is 4 points ahead of Luigi Fagioli.
