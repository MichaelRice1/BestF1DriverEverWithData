The 1986 Detroit Grand Prix started Jun 22, 1986 on Detroit Street Circuit in Detroit, United States of America. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 51 minutes and 12 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacques Laffite in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1986 Detroit Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 36 points. Senna is 3 points ahead of Alain Prost.
