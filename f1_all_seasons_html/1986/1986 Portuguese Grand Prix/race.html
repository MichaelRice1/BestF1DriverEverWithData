The 1986 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 21, 1986 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 21 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1986 Portuguese Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Mansell is 10 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
