The 1986 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 23, 1986 on Autódromo Internacional Nelson Piquet in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 32 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Jacques Laffite in third place.

After the 1986 Brazilian Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Piquet is 3 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
