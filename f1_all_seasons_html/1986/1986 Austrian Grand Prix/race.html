The 1986 Austrian Grand Prix started Aug 17, 1986 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Teo Fabi started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Keke Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 21 minutes and 22 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michele Alboreto in second and Stefan Johansson in third place.

After the 1986 Austrian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 55 points. Mansell is 2 points ahead of Alain Prost.
