The 1986 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 27, 1986 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 28 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1986 San Marino Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Senna is 0 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
