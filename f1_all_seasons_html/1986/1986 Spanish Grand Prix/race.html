The 1986 Spanish Grand Prix started Apr 13, 1986 on Circuito de Jerez in Jerez de la Frontera, Spain. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 48 minutes and 47 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1986 Spanish Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Senna is 6 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
