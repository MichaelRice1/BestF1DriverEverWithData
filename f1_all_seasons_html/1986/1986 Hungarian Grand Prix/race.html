The 1986 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 10, 1986 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 34 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Nigel Mansell in third place.

After the 1986 Hungarian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 55 points. Mansell is 7 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
