The 2019 German Grand Prix started Jul 28, 2019 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 31 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Daniil Kvyat in third place.

After the 2019 German Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 225 points. Hamilton is 41 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
