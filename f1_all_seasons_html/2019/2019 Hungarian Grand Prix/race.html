The 2019 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 4, 2019 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 03 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2019 Hungarian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 250 points. Hamilton is 62 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
