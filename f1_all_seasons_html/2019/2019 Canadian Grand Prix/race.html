The 2019 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 9, 2019 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Charles Leclerc started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 07 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2019 Canadian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 162 points. Hamilton is 29 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
