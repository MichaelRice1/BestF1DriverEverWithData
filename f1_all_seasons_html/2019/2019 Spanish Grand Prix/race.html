The 2019 Spanish Grand Prix started May 12, 2019 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 50 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2019 Spanish Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 112 points. Hamilton is 7 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
