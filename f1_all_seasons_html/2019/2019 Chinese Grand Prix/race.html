The 2019 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 14, 2019 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 06 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2019 Chinese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 68 points. Hamilton is 6 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
