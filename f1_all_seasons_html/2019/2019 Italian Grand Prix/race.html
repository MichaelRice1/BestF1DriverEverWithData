The 2019 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 8, 2019 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 15 minutes and 26 seconds Charles Leclerc finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2019 Italian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 284 points. Hamilton is 63 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
