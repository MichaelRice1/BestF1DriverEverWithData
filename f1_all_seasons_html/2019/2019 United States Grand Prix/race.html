The 2019 United States Grand Prix started Nov 3, 2019 on Circuit of the Americas in Austin, United States of America. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 55 seconds Valtteri Bottas finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2019 United States Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 381 points. Hamilton is 67 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
