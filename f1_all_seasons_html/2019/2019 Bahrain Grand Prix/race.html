The 2019 Bahrain Grand Prix started Mar 31, 2019 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 21 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2019 Bahrain Grand Prix, Valtteri Bottas is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 44 points. Bottas is 1 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
