The 2019 Brazilian Grand Prix started Nov 17, 2019 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 14 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Pierre Gasly in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2019 Brazilian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 387 points. Hamilton is 73 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
