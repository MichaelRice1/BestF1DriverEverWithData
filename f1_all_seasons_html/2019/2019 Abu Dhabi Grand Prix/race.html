The 2019 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Dec 1, 2019 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 05 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2019 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 413 points. Hamilton is 87 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
