The 2019 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 27, 2019 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 48 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2019 Mexican Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 363 points. Hamilton is 74 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
