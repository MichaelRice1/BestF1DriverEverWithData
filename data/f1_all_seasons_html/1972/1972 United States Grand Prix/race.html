The 1972 United States Grand Prix started Oct 8, 1972 on Watkins Glen International in Watkins Glen, United States of America. Jackie Stewart started from pole position with Peter Revson next to him. Denny Hulme started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 45 seconds Jackie Stewart finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with François Cevert in second and Denny Hulme in third place.

After the 1972 United States Grand Prix, Emerson Fittipaldi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 61 points. Fittipaldi is 16 points ahead of Jackie Stewart.
