The 1952 Swiss Grand Prix started May 18, 1952 on Circuit Bremgarten in Bern, Switzerland. Nino Farina started from pole position with Piero Taruffi next to him. Robert Manzon started from third place.

In 3 hour, 01 minutes and 46 seconds Piero Taruffi finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rudi Fischer in second and Jean Behra in third place.

After the 1952 Swiss Grand Prix, Piero Taruffi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Taruffi is 3 points ahead of Rudi Fischer.
