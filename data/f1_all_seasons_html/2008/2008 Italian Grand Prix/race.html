The 2008 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 14, 2008 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Heikki Kovalainen next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 47 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Heikki Kovalainen in second and Robert Kubica in third place.

After the 2008 Italian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 78 points. Hamilton is 1 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
