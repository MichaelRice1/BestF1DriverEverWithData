The 2008 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 12, 2008 on Fuji Speedway in Oyama, Japan. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Heikki Kovalainen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 21 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Robert Kubica in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2008 Japanese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 84 points. Hamilton is 5 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
