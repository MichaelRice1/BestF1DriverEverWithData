The 2008 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 16, 2008 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Robert Kubica next to him. Heikki Kovalainen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 50 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nick Heidfeld in second and Nico Rosberg in third place.

After the 2008 Australian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Hamilton is 2 points ahead of Nick Heidfeld.
