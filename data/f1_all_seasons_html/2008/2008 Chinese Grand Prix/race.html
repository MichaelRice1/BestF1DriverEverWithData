The 2008 Chinese Grand Prix started Oct 19, 2008 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 57 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Felipe Massa in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2008 Chinese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 94 points. Hamilton is 7 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
