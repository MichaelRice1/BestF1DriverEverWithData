The 2008 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 28, 2008 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 57 minutes and 16 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2008 Singapore Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 84 points. Hamilton is 7 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
