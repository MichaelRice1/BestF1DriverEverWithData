The 2008 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 23, 2008 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Heikki Kovalainen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 18 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Robert Kubica in second and Heikki Kovalainen in third place.

After the 2008 Malaysian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 14 points. Hamilton is 3 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
