The 2021 Mexican Grand Prix started Nov 7, 2021 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 39 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2021 Mexican Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 312.5 points. Verstappen is 19 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
