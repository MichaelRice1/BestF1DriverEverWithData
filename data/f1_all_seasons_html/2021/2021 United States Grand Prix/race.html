The 2021 United States Grand Prix started Oct 24, 2021 on Circuit of the Americas in Austin, United States of America. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 36 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2021 United States Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 287.5 points. Verstappen is 12 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
