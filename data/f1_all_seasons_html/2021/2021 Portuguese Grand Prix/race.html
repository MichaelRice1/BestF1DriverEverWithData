The 2021 Portuguese Grand Prix started May 2, 2021 on Algarve International Circuit in Portimão, Portugal. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 31 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2021 Portuguese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 69 points. Hamilton is 8 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
