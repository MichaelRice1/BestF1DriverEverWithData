The 2021 Dutch Grand Prix started Sep 5, 2021 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 05 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2021 Dutch Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 224.5 points. Verstappen is 3 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
