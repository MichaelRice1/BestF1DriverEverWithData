The 2021 Austrian Grand Prix started Jul 4, 2021 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Lando Norris next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 23 minutes and 54 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Lando Norris in third place.

After the 2021 Austrian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 182 points. Verstappen is 32 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
