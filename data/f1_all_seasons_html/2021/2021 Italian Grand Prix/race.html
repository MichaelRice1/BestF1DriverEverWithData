The 2021 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 12, 2021 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 21 minutes and 54 seconds Daniel Ricciardo finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2021 Italian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 226.5 points. Verstappen is 5 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
