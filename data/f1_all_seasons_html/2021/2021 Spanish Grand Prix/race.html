The 2021 Spanish Grand Prix started May 9, 2021 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 07 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2021 Spanish Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 94 points. Hamilton is 14 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
