The 2021 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 29, 2021 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Max Verstappen started from pole position with George Russell next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 3 minutes and 27 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with George Russell in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2021 Belgian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 202.5 points. Hamilton is 3 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
