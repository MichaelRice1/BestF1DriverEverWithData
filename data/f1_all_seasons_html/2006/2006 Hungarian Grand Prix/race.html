The 2006 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 6, 2006 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Felipe Massa next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 52 minutes and 20 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Pedro de la Rosa in second and Nick Heidfeld in third place.

After the 2006 Hungarian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 100 points. Alonso is 10 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
