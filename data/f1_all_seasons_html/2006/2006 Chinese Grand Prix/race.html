The 2006 Chinese Grand Prix started Oct 1, 2006 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Giancarlo Fisichella next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

After the 2006 Chinese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 116 points. Schumacher is 0 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
