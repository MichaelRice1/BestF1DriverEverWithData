The 2006 United States Grand Prix started Jul 2, 2006 on Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Indianapolis, United States of America. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Felipe Massa next to him. Giancarlo Fisichella started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 35 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Felipe Massa in second and Giancarlo Fisichella in third place.

After the 2006 United States Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 88 points. Alonso is 19 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
