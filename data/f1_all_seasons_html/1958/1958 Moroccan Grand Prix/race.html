The 1958 Moroccan Grand Prix started Oct 19, 1958 on Ain-Diab Circuit in Casablanca, Morocco. Mike Hawthorn started from pole position with Stirling Moss next to him. Stuart Lewis-Evans started from third place.

In 2 hour, 09 minutes and 15 seconds Stirling Moss finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mike Hawthorn in second and Phil Hill in third place.

After the 1958 Moroccan Grand Prix, Mike Hawthorn is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 42 points. Hawthorn is 1 points ahead of Stirling Moss.
