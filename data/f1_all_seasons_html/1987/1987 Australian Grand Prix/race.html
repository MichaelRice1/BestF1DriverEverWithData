The 1987 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 15, 1987 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 52 minutes and 56 seconds Gerhard Berger finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michele Alboreto in second and Thierry Boutsen in third place.

After the 1987 Australian Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 73 points. Piquet is 12 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
