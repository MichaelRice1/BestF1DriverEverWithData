The 1987 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 9, 1987 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 59 minutes and 26 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1987 Hungarian Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 48 points. Piquet is 7 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
