The 1987 Belgian Grand Prix started May 17, 1987 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 03 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Stefan Johansson in second and Andrea de Cesaris in third place.

After the 1987 Belgian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Prost is 5 points ahead of Stefan Johansson.
