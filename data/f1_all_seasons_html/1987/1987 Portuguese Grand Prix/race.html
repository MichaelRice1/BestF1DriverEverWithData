The 1987 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 20, 1987 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 03 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1987 Portuguese Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 67 points. Piquet is 18 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
