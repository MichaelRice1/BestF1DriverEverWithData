The 1986 British Grand Prix started Jul 13, 1986 on Brands Hatch in Fawkham, United Kingdom. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 38 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1986 British Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 47 points. Mansell is 4 points ahead of Alain Prost.
