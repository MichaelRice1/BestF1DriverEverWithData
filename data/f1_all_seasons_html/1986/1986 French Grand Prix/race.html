The 1986 French Grand Prix started Jul 6, 1986 on Circuit Paul Ricard in Le Castellet, France. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 19 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1986 French Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 39 points. Prost is 1 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
