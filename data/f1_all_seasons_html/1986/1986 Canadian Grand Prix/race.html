The 1986 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 15, 1986 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 26 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1986 Canadian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 29 points. Prost is 2 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
