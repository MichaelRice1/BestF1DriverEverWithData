The 1986 Australian Grand Prix started Oct 26, 1986 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 54 minutes and 20 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Stefan Johansson in third place.

After the 1986 Australian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Prost is 2 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
