The 1986 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 12, 1986 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 18 seconds Gerhard Berger finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1986 Mexican Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Mansell is 6 points ahead of Alain Prost.
