The 2007 Spanish Grand Prix started May 13, 2007 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 36 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2007 Spanish Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Hamilton is 2 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
