The 2007 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 15, 2007 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 27 seconds Felipe Massa finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2007 Bahrain Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 22 points. Alonso is 0 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
