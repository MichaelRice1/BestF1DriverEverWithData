The 2007 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 18, 2007 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Nick Heidfeld started from third place.

In 1 hour, 25 minutes and 28 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2007 Australian Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Räikkönen is 2 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
