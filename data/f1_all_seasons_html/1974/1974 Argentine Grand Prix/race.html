The 1974 Argentine Grand Prix started Jan 13, 1974 on Autódromo Juan y Oscar Gálvez in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Ronnie Peterson started from pole position with Clay Regazzoni next to him. Emerson Fittipaldi started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 02 seconds Denny Hulme finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and Clay Regazzoni in third place.

After the 1974 Argentine Grand Prix, Denny Hulme is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Hulme is 3 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
