The 1974 Swedish Grand Prix started Jun 9, 1974 on Anderstorp Raceway in Anderstorp, Sweden. Patrick Depailler started from pole position with Jody Scheckter next to him. Niki Lauda started from third place.

In 1 hour, 58 minutes and 31 seconds Jody Scheckter finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Depailler in second and James Hunt in third place.

After the 1974 Swedish Grand Prix, Emerson Fittipaldi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 27 points. Fittipaldi is 5 points ahead of Clay Regazzoni.
