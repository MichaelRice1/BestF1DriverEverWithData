The 1974 Canadian Grand Prix started Sep 22, 1974 on Canadian Tire Motorsport Park in Bowmanville, Canada. Emerson Fittipaldi started from pole position with Niki Lauda next to him. Jody Scheckter started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 26 seconds Emerson Fittipaldi finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Clay Regazzoni in second and Ronnie Peterson in third place.

After the 1974 Canadian Grand Prix, Emerson Fittipaldi is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 52 points. Fittipaldi is 0 points ahead of Clay Regazzoni.
