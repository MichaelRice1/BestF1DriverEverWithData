The 1992 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 8, 1992 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Riccardo Patrese started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 54 seconds Gerhard Berger finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Martin Brundle in third place.

After the 1992 Australian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 108 points. Mansell is 52 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
