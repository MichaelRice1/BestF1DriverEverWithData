The 1992 Spanish Grand Prix started May 3, 1992 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 56 minutes and 10 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1992 Spanish Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Mansell is 22 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
