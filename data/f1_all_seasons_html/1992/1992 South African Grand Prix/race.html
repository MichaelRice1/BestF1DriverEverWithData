The 1992 South African Grand Prix started Mar 1, 1992 on Kyalami Racing Circuit in Midrand, South Africa. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 45 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1992 South African Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Mansell is 4 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
