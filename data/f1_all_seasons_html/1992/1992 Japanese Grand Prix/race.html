The 1992 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 25, 1992 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 09 seconds Riccardo Patrese finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Martin Brundle in third place.

After the 1992 Japanese Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 108 points. Mansell is 52 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
