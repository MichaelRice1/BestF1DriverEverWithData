The 1992 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 14, 1992 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 08 seconds Gerhard Berger finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1992 Canadian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 56 points. Mansell is 28 points ahead of Riccardo Patrese.
