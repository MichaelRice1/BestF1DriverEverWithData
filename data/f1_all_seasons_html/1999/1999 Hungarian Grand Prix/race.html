The 1999 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 15, 1999 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Eddie Irvine next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 23 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Eddie Irvine in third place.

After the 1999 Hungarian Grand Prix, Eddie Irvine is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 56 points. Irvine is 2 points ahead of Mika Häkkinen.
