The 1999 Spanish Grand Prix started May 30, 1999 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Eddie Irvine next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 13 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1999 Spanish Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Schumacher is 6 points ahead of Mika Häkkinen.
