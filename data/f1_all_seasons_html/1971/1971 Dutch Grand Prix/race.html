The 1971 Dutch Grand Prix started Jun 20, 1971 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Jacky Ickx started from pole position with Pedro Rodríguez next to him. Jackie Stewart started from third place.

In 1 hour, 56 minutes and 20 seconds Jacky Ickx finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Pedro Rodríguez in second and Clay Regazzoni in third place.

After the 1971 Dutch Grand Prix, Jackie Stewart is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Stewart is 5 points ahead of Jacky Ickx.
