The 1980 United States Grand Prix West started Mar 30, 1980 on Long Beach in Long Beach, United States of America. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with René Arnoux next to him. Patrick Depailler started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 18 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Emerson Fittipaldi in third place.

After the 1980 United States Grand Prix West, René Arnoux is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 18 points. Arnoux is 0 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
