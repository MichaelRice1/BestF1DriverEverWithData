The 1980 Argentine Grand Prix started Jan 13, 1980 on Autódromo Juan y Oscar Gálvez in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Alan Jones started from pole position with Jacques Laffite next to him. Didier Pironi started from third place.

In 1 hour, 43 minutes and 24 seconds Alan Jones finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Keke Rosberg in third place.

After the 1980 Argentine Grand Prix, Alan Jones is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Jones is 3 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
