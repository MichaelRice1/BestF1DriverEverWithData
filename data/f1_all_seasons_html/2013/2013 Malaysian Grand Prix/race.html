The 2013 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 24, 2013 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Felipe Massa next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 56 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2013 Malaysian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Vettel is 9 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
