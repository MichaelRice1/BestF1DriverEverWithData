The 2013 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 13, 2013 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Mark Webber started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 49 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Romain Grosjean in third place.

After the 2013 Japanese Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 297 points. Vettel is 90 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
