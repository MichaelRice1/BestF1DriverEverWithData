The 2013 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 21, 2013 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 00 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Romain Grosjean in third place.

After the 2013 Bahrain Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 77 points. Vettel is 10 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
