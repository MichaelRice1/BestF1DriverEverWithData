The 1994 German Grand Prix started Jul 31, 1994 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Jean Alesi next to him. Damon Hill started from third place.

In 1 hour, 22 minutes and 37 seconds Gerhard Berger finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Olivier Panis in second and Éric Bernard in third place.

After the 1994 German Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 66 points. Schumacher is 27 points ahead of Damon Hill.
