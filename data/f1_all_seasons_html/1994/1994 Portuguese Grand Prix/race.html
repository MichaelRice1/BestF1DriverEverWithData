The 1994 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 25, 1994 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 10 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Mika Häkkinen in third place.

After the 1994 Portuguese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Schumacher is 1 points ahead of Damon Hill.
