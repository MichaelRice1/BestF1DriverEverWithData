The 1994 European Grand Prix started Oct 16, 1994 on Circuito de Jerez in Jerez de la Frontera, Spain. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 26 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Mika Häkkinen in third place.

After the 1994 European Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 86 points. Schumacher is 5 points ahead of Damon Hill.
