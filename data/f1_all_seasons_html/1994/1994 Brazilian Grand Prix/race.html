The 1994 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 27, 1994 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Jean Alesi started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 38 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1994 Brazilian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Schumacher is 4 points ahead of Damon Hill.
