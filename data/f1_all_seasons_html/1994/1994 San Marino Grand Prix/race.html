The 1994 San Marino Grand Prix started May 1, 1994 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 28 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nicola Larini in second and Mika Häkkinen in third place.

After the 1994 San Marino Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Schumacher is 23 points ahead of Damon Hill.
