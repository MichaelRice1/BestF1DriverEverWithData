The 1994 Monaco Grand Prix started May 15, 1994 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mika Häkkinen next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 55 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Martin Brundle in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1994 Monaco Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Schumacher is 30 points ahead of Gerhard Berger.
