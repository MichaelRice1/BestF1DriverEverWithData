The 1994 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 13, 1994 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Damon Hill started from third place.

In 1 hour, 47 minutes and 51 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Martin Brundle in third place.

After the 1994 Australian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 92 points. Schumacher is 1 points ahead of Damon Hill.
