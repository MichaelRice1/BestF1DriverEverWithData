The 1967 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 22, 1967 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Jim Clark started from pole position with Chris Amon next to him. Dan Gurney started from third place.

In 1 hour, 59 minutes and 28 seconds Jim Clark finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jack Brabham in second and Denny Hulme in third place.

After the 1967 Mexican Grand Prix, Denny Hulme is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 51 points. Hulme is 5 points ahead of Jack Brabham.
