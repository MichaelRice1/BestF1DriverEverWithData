The 2022 Azerbaijan Grand Prix started Jun 12, 2022 on Baku City Circuit in Baku, Azerbaijan. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 05 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2022 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 150 points. Verstappen is 21 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
