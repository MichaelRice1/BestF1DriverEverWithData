The 2022 Australian Grand Prix started Apr 10, 2022 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 46 seconds Charles Leclerc finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2022 Australian Grand Prix, Charles Leclerc is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 71 points. Leclerc is 34 points ahead of George Russell.
