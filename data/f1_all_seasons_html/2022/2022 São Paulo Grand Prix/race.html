The 2022 São Paulo Grand Prix started Nov 13, 2022 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Kevin Magnussen started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. George Russell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 34 seconds George Russell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2022 São Paulo Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 429 points. Verstappen is 139 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
