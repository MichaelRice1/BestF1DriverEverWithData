The 2022 Singapore Grand Prix started Oct 2, 2022 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 2 hour, 02 minutes and 20 seconds Sergio Pérez finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2022 Singapore Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 341 points. Verstappen is 104 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
