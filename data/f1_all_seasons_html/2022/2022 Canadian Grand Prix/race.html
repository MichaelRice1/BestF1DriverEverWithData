The 2022 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 19, 2022 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 21 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Sainz Jr. in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2022 Canadian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 175 points. Verstappen is 46 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
