The 2022 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 30, 2022 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Max Verstappen started from pole position with George Russell next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 36 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2022 Mexican Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 416 points. Verstappen is 136 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
