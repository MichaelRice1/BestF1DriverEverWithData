The 2022 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix started Apr 24, 2022 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Lando Norris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 07 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Lando Norris in third place.

After the 2022 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix, Charles Leclerc is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 86 points. Leclerc is 27 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
