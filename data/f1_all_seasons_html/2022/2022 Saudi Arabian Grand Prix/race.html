The 2022 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix started Mar 27, 2022 on Jeddah Corniche Circuit in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Sergio Pérez started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 19 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2022 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix, Charles Leclerc is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 45 points. Leclerc is 12 points ahead of Carlos Sainz Jr..
