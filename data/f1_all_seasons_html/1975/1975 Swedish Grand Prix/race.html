The 1975 Swedish Grand Prix started Jun 8, 1975 on Anderstorp Raceway in Anderstorp, Sweden. Vittorio Brambilla started from pole position with Patrick Depailler next to him. Jean-Pierre Jarier started from third place.

In 1 hour, 59 minutes and 18 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Carlos Reutemann in second and Clay Regazzoni in third place.

After the 1975 Swedish Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 32 points. Lauda is 10 points ahead of Carlos Reutemann.
