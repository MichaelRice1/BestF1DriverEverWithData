The 2023 Dutch Grand Prix started Aug 27, 2023 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Lando Norris next to him. George Russell started from third place.

In 2 hour, 24 minutes and 04 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Pierre Gasly in third place.

After the 2023 Dutch Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 339 points. Verstappen is 138 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
