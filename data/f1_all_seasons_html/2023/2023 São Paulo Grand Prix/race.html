The 2023 São Paulo Grand Prix started Nov 5, 2023 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Lance Stroll started from third place.

In 1 hour, 56 minutes and 48 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2023 São Paulo Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 524 points. Verstappen is 266 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
