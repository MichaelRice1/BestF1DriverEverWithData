The 2023 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 26, 2023 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Oscar Piastri started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 02 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2023 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 575 points. Verstappen is 290 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
