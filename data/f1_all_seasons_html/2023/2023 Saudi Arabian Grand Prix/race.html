The 2023 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix started Mar 19, 2023 on Jeddah Corniche Circuit in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Sergio Pérez started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 21 minutes and 14 seconds Sergio Pérez finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2023 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 44 points. Verstappen is 1 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
