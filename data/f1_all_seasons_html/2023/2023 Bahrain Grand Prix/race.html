The 2023 Bahrain Grand Prix started Mar 5, 2023 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Charles Leclerc started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 56 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2023 Bahrain Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 25 points. Verstappen is 7 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
