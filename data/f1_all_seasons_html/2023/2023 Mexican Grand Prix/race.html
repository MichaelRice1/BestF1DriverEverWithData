The 2023 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 29, 2023 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Carlos Sainz Jr. next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 2 hour, 02 minutes and 30 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2023 Mexican Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 491 points. Verstappen is 251 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
