The 2015 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 6, 2015 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 18 minutes and 00 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2015 Italian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 252 points. Hamilton is 53 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
