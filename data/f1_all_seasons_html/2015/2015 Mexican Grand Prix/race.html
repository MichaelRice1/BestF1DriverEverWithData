The 2015 Mexican Grand Prix started Nov 1, 2015 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 35 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2015 Mexican Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 345 points. Hamilton is 73 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
