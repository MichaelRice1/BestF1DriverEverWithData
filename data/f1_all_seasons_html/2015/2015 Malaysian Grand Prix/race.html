The 2015 Malaysian Grand Prix started Mar 29, 2015 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 05 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Nico Rosberg in third place.

After the 2015 Malaysian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 43 points. Hamilton is 3 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
