The 2015 Monaco Grand Prix started May 24, 2015 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 18 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2015 Monaco Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 126 points. Hamilton is 10 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
