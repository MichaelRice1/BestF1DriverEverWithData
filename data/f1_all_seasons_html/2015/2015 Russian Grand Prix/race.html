The 2015 Russian Grand Prix started Oct 11, 2015 on Sochi Autodrom in Sochi, Russia. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 11 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2015 Russian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 302 points. Hamilton is 66 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
