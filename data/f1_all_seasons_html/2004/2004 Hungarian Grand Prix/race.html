The 2004 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 15, 2004 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Takuma Sato started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 26 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2004 Hungarian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 120 points. Schumacher is 38 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
