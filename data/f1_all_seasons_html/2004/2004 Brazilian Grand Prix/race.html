The 2004 Brazilian Grand Prix started Oct 24, 2004 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Rubens Barrichello started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 01 seconds Juan Pablo Montoya finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2004 Brazilian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 148 points. Schumacher is 34 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
