The 2004 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 10, 2004 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Ralf Schumacher next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 26 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ralf Schumacher in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2004 Japanese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 146 points. Schumacher is 38 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
