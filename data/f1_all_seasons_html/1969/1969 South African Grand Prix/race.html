The 1969 South African Grand Prix started Mar 1, 1969 on Kyalami Racing Circuit in Midrand, South Africa. Jack Brabham started from pole position with Jochen Rindt next to him. Denny Hulme started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 39 seconds Jackie Stewart finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Graham Hill in second and Denny Hulme in third place.

After the 1969 South African Grand Prix, Jackie Stewart is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Stewart is 3 points ahead of Graham Hill.
