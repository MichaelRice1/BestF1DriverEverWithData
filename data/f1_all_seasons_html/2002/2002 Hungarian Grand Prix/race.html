The 2002 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 18, 2002 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Rubens Barrichello started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Ralf Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 49 seconds Rubens Barrichello finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 2002 Hungarian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 112 points. Schumacher is 67 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
