The 2002 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 14, 2002 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Ralf Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 10 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Ralf Schumacher in third place.

After the 2002 San Marino Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 34 points. Schumacher is 14 points ahead of Ralf Schumacher.
