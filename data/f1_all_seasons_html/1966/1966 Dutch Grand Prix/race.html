The 1966 Dutch Grand Prix started Jul 24, 1966 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Jack Brabham started from pole position with Denny Hulme next to him. Jim Clark started from third place.

In 2 hour, 20 minutes and 32 seconds Jack Brabham finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Graham Hill in second and Jim Clark in third place.

After the 1966 Dutch Grand Prix, Jack Brabham is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Brabham is 16 points ahead of Graham Hill.
