The 1991 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 24, 1991 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 28 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1991 Brazilian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Senna is 11 points ahead of Alain Prost.
