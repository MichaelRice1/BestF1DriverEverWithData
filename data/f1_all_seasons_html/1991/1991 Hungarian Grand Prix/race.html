The 1991 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 11, 1991 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 12 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1991 Hungarian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 61 points. Senna is 12 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
