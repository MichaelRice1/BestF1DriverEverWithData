The 1991 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 3, 1991 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 24 minutes and 34 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1991 Australian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 96 points. Senna is 24 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
