The 1991 United States Grand Prix started Mar 10, 1991 on Phoenix Street Circuit in Phoenix, United States of America. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Riccardo Patrese started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 47 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1991 United States Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Senna is 4 points ahead of Alain Prost.
