The 1991 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 28, 1991 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 14 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and JJ Lehto in third place.

After the 1991 San Marino Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Senna is 20 points ahead of Gerhard Berger.
