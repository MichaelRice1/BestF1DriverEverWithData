The 1991 Spanish Grand Prix started Sep 29, 1991 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 41 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1991 Spanish Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 85 points. Senna is 16 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
