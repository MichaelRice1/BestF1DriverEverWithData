The 1991 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 20, 1991 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Gerhard Berger started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 10 seconds Gerhard Berger finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1991 Japanese Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 91 points. Senna is 22 points ahead of Nigel Mansell.
