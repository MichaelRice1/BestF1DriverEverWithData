The 2005 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 24, 2005 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 41 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Alexander Wurz in third place.

After the 2005 San Marino Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 36 points. Alonso is 16 points ahead of Jarno Trulli.
