The 2005 Turkish Grand Prix started Aug 21, 2005 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Giancarlo Fisichella next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 34 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Juan Pablo Montoya in third place.

After the 2005 Turkish Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 95 points. Alonso is 24 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
