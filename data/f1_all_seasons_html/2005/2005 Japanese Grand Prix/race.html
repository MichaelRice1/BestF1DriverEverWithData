The 2005 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 9, 2005 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Ralf Schumacher started from pole position with Jenson Button next to him. Giancarlo Fisichella started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 02 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Giancarlo Fisichella in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2005 Japanese Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 123 points. Alonso is 19 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
