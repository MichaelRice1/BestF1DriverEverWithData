The 2005 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 12, 2005 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Jenson Button started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 09 seconds Kimi Räikkönen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2005 Canadian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 59 points. Alonso is 22 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
