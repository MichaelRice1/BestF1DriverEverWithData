The 2005 European Grand Prix started May 29, 2005 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Nick Heidfeld started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 46 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nick Heidfeld in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2005 European Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 59 points. Alonso is 32 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
