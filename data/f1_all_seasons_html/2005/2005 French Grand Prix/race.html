The 2005 French Grand Prix started Jul 3, 2005 on Circuit de Nevers Magny-Cours in Nevers, France. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Jarno Trulli next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 22 seconds Fernando Alonso finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 2005 French Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 69 points. Alonso is 24 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
