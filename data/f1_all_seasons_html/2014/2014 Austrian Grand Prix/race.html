The 2014 Austrian Grand Prix started Jun 22, 2014 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Felipe Massa started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Nico Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 54 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2014 Austrian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 165 points. Rosberg is 29 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
