The 2014 Brazilian Grand Prix started Nov 9, 2014 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Felipe Massa started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 02 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2014 Brazilian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 334 points. Hamilton is 17 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
