The 2014 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 8, 2014 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 12 seconds Daniel Ricciardo finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2014 Canadian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 140 points. Rosberg is 22 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
