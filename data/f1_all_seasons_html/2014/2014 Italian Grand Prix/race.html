The 2014 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 7, 2014 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 19 minutes and 10 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2014 Italian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 238 points. Rosberg is 22 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
