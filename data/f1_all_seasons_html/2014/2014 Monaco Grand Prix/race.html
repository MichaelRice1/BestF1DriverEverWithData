The 2014 Monaco Grand Prix started May 25, 2014 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 27 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2014 Monaco Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 122 points. Rosberg is 4 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
