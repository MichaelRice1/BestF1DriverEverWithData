The 2014 Russian Grand Prix started Oct 21, 2014 on Sochi Autodrom in Sochi, Russia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 50 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2014 Russian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 291 points. Hamilton is 17 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
