The 2014 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 21, 2014 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 04 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2014 Singapore Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 241 points. Hamilton is 3 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
