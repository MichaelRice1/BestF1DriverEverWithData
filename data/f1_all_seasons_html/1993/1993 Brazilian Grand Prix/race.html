The 1993 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 28, 1993 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 51 minutes and 15 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1993 Brazilian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 16 points. Senna is 6 points ahead of Alain Prost.
