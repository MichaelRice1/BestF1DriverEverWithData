The 1993 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 7, 1993 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Damon Hill started from third place.

In 1 hour, 43 minutes and 27 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Damon Hill in third place.

After the 1993 Australian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 99 points. Prost is 26 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
