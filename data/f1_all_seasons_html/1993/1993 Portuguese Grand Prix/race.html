The 1993 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 26, 1993 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Damon Hill started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 46 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Damon Hill in third place.

After the 1993 Portuguese Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 87 points. Prost is 25 points ahead of Damon Hill.
