The 1993 Spanish Grand Prix started May 9, 1993 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 27 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1993 Spanish Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 34 points. Prost is 2 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
