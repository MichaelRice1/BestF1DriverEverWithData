The 1993 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 24, 1993 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Alain Prost started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 27 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Mika Häkkinen in third place.

After the 1993 Japanese Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 93 points. Prost is 28 points ahead of Damon Hill.
