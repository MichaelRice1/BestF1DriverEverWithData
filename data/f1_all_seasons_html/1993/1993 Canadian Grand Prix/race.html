The 1993 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 13, 1993 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Alain Prost started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 41 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Damon Hill in third place.

After the 1993 Canadian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 47 points. Prost is 5 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
