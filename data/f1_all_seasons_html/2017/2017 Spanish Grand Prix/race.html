The 2017 Spanish Grand Prix started May 14, 2017 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 56 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2017 Spanish Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 104 points. Vettel is 6 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
