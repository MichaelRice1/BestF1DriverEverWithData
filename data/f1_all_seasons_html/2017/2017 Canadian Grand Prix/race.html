The 2017 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 11, 2017 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 05 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2017 Canadian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 141 points. Vettel is 12 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
