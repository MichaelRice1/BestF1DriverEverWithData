The 2017 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 9, 2017 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 36 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2017 Chinese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 43 points. Hamilton is 0 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
