The 2017 Azerbaijan Grand Prix started Jun 25, 2017 on Baku City Circuit in Baku, Azerbaijan. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 2 hour, 03 minutes and 55 seconds Daniel Ricciardo finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Lance Stroll in third place.

After the 2017 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 153 points. Vettel is 14 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
