The 2017 Monaco Grand Prix started May 28, 2017 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 44 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2017 Monaco Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 129 points. Vettel is 25 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
