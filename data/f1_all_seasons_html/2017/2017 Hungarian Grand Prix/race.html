The 2017 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 30, 2017 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 46 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2017 Hungarian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 202 points. Vettel is 14 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
