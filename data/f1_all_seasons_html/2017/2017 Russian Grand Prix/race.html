The 2017 Russian Grand Prix started Apr 30, 2017 on Sochi Autodrom in Sochi, Russia. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 08 seconds Valtteri Bottas finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2017 Russian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 86 points. Vettel is 13 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
