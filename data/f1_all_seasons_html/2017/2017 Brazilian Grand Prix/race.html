The 2017 Brazilian Grand Prix started Nov 12, 2017 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 26 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2017 Brazilian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 345 points. Hamilton is 43 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
