The 2017 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 26, 2017 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 14 seconds Valtteri Bottas finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2017 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 363 points. Hamilton is 46 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
