The 2017 Bahrain Grand Prix started Apr 16, 2017 on Bahrain International Circuit in Sakhir, Bahrain. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 53 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2017 Bahrain Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 68 points. Vettel is 7 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
