The 2011 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 25, 2011 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 59 minutes and 06 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2011 Singapore Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 309 points. Vettel is 124 points ahead of Jenson Button.
