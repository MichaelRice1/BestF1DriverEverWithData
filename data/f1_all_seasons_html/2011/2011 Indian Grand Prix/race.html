The 2011 Indian Grand Prix started Oct 30, 2011 on Buddh International Circuit in Greater Noida, India. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 35 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2011 Indian Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 374 points. Vettel is 134 points ahead of Jenson Button.
