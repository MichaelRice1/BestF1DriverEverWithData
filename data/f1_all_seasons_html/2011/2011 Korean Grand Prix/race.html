The 2011 Korean Grand Prix started Oct 16, 2011 on Korea International Circuit in Yeongam, South Korea. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Jenson Button started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 01 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2011 Korean Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 349 points. Vettel is 127 points ahead of Jenson Button.
