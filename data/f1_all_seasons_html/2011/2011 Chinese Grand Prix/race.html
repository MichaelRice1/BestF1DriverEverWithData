The 2011 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 17, 2011 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Jenson Button next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 58 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2011 Chinese Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 68 points. Vettel is 21 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
