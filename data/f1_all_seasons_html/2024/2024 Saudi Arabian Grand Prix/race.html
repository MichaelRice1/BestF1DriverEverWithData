The 2024 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix started Mar 9, 2024 on Jeddah Corniche Circuit in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Charles Leclerc next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 20 minutes and 43 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2024 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 51 points. Verstappen is 15 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
