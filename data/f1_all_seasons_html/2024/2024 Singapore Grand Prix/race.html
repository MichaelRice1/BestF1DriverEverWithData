The 2024 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 22, 2024 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Lando Norris started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 52 seconds Lando Norris finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Oscar Piastri in third place.

After the 2024 Singapore Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 331 points. Verstappen is 52 points ahead of Lando Norris.
