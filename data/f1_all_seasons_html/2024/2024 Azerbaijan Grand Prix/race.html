The 2024 Azerbaijan Grand Prix started Sep 15, 2024 on Baku City Circuit in Baku, Azerbaijan. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Oscar Piastri next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 58 seconds Oscar Piastri finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and George Russell in third place.

After the 2024 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 313 points. Verstappen is 59 points ahead of Lando Norris.
