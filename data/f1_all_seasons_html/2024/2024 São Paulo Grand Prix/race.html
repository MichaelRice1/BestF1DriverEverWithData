The 2024 São Paulo Grand Prix started Nov 3, 2024 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Lando Norris started from pole position with George Russell next to him. Yuki Tsunoda started from third place.

In 2 hour, 06 minutes and 54 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Esteban Ocon in second and Pierre Gasly in third place.

After the 2024 São Paulo Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 393 points. Verstappen is 62 points ahead of Lando Norris.
