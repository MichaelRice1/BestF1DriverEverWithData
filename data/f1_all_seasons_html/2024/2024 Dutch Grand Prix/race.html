The 2024 Dutch Grand Prix started Aug 25, 2024 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Lando Norris started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Oscar Piastri started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 45 seconds Lando Norris finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2024 Dutch Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 295 points. Verstappen is 70 points ahead of Lando Norris.
