The 2024 Japanese Grand Prix started Apr 7, 2024 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Lando Norris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 54 minutes and 23 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Carlos Sainz Jr. in third place.

After the 2024 Japanese Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 77 points. Verstappen is 13 points ahead of Sergio Pérez.
