The 2024 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix started May 19, 2024 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Oscar Piastri next to him. Lando Norris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 25 minutes and 25 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2024 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 161 points. Verstappen is 48 points ahead of Charles Leclerc.
