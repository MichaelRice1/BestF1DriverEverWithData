The 2024 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 21, 2024 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Lando Norris started from pole position with Oscar Piastri next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 01 seconds Oscar Piastri finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2024 Hungarian Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 265 points. Verstappen is 76 points ahead of Lando Norris.
