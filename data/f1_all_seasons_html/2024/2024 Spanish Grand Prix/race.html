The 2024 Spanish Grand Prix started Jun 23, 2024 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Lando Norris started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 20 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lando Norris in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2024 Spanish Grand Prix, Max Verstappen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 219 points. Verstappen is 69 points ahead of Lando Norris.
