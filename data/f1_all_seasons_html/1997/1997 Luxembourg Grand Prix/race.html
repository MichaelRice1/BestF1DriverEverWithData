The 1997 Luxembourg Grand Prix started Sep 28, 1997 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Mika Häkkinen started from pole position with Jacques Villeneuve next to him. Heinz-Harald Frentzen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 27 seconds Jacques Villeneuve finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and Heinz-Harald Frentzen in third place.

After the 1997 Luxembourg Grand Prix, Jacques Villeneuve is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 77 points. Villeneuve is 9 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
