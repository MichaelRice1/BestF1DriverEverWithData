The 1996 German Grand Prix started Jul 28, 1996 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Damon Hill started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 21 minutes and 43 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and Jacques Villeneuve in third place.

After the 1996 German Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 73 points. Hill is 21 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
