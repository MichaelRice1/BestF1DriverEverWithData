The 1996 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 31, 1996 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Damon Hill started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Jacques Villeneuve started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 52 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and Michael Schumacher in third place.

After the 1996 Brazilian Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Hill is 14 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
