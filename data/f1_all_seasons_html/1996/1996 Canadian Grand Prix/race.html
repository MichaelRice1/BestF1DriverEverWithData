The 1996 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 16, 1996 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Damon Hill started from pole position with Jacques Villeneuve next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 03 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacques Villeneuve in second and Jean Alesi in third place.

After the 1996 Canadian Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 53 points. Hill is 21 points ahead of Jacques Villeneuve.
