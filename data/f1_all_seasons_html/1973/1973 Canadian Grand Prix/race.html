The 1973 Canadian Grand Prix started Sep 23, 1973 on Canadian Tire Motorsport Park in Bowmanville, Canada. Ronnie Peterson started from pole position with Peter Revson next to him. Jody Scheckter started from third place.

In 1 hour, 59 minutes and 04 seconds Peter Revson finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Emerson Fittipaldi in second and Jackie Oliver in third place.

After the 1973 Canadian Grand Prix, Jackie Stewart is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 71 points. Stewart is 17 points ahead of Emerson Fittipaldi.
