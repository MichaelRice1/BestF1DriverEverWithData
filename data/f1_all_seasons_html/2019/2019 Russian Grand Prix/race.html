The 2019 Russian Grand Prix started Sep 29, 2019 on Sochi Autodrom in Sochi, Russia. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 38 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2019 Russian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 322 points. Hamilton is 73 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
