The 2019 Azerbaijan Grand Prix started Apr 28, 2019 on Baku City Circuit in Baku, Azerbaijan. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 52 seconds Valtteri Bottas finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2019 Azerbaijan Grand Prix, Valtteri Bottas is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 87 points. Bottas is 1 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
