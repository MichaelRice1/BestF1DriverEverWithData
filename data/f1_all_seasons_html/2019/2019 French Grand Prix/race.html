The 2019 French Grand Prix started Jun 23, 2019 on Circuit Paul Ricard in Le Castellet, France. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Charles Leclerc started from third place.

In 1 hour, 24 minutes and 31 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Charles Leclerc in third place.

After the 2019 French Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 187 points. Hamilton is 36 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
