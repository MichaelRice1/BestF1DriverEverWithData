The 2019 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 22, 2019 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 58 minutes and 33 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2019 Singapore Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 296 points. Hamilton is 65 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
