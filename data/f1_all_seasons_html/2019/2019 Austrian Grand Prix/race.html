The 2019 Austrian Grand Prix started Jun 30, 2019 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Charles Leclerc started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 22 minutes and 01 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Charles Leclerc in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2019 Austrian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 197 points. Hamilton is 31 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
