The 1995 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 26, 1995 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Damon Hill started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 34 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1995 Brazilian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Schumacher is 4 points ahead of David Coulthard.
