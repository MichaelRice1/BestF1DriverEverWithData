The 1995 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 12, 1995 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Damon Hill started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 49 minutes and 15 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Olivier Panis in second and Gianni Morbidelli in third place.

After the 1995 Australian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 102 points. Schumacher is 33 points ahead of Damon Hill.
