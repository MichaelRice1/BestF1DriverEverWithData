The 1995 Pacific Grand Prix started Oct 22, 1995 on Okayama International Circuit in Aida, Japan. David Coulthard started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 48 minutes and 49 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Damon Hill in third place.

After the 1995 Pacific Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 92 points. Schumacher is 33 points ahead of Damon Hill.
