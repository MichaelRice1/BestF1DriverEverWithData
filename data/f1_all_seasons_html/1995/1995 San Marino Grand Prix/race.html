The 1995 San Marino Grand Prix started Apr 30, 1995 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 42 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jean Alesi in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1995 San Marino Grand Prix, Damon Hill is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 20 points. Hill is 6 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
