The 1995 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 24, 1995 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. David Coulthard started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 52 seconds David Coulthard finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Damon Hill in third place.

After the 1995 Portuguese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Schumacher is 17 points ahead of Damon Hill.
