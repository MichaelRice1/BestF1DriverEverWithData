The 1995 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 11, 1995 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Damon Hill next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 54 seconds Jean Alesi finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Eddie Irvine in third place.

After the 1995 Canadian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 36 points. Schumacher is 7 points ahead of Damon Hill.
