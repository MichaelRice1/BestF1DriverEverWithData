The 1995 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 13, 1995 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Damon Hill started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 25 seconds Damon Hill finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with David Coulthard in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1995 Hungarian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 56 points. Schumacher is 11 points ahead of Damon Hill.
