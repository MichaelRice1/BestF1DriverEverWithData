The 1995 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 29, 1995 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Jean Alesi next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 52 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mika Häkkinen in second and Johnny Herbert in third place.

After the 1995 Japanese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 102 points. Schumacher is 43 points ahead of Damon Hill.
