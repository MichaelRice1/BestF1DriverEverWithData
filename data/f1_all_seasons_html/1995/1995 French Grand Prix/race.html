The 1995 French Grand Prix started Jul 2, 1995 on Circuit de Nevers Magny-Cours in Nevers, France. Damon Hill started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 28 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Damon Hill in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 1995 French Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 46 points. Schumacher is 11 points ahead of Damon Hill.
