The 2001 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 14, 2001 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Ralf Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 33 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2001 Japanese Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 123 points. Schumacher is 58 points ahead of David Coulthard.
