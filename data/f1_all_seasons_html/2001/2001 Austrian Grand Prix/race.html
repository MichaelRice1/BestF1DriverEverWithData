The 2001 Austrian Grand Prix started May 13, 2001 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Juan Pablo Montoya next to him. Ralf Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 45 seconds David Coulthard finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2001 Austrian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 42 points. Schumacher is 4 points ahead of David Coulthard.
