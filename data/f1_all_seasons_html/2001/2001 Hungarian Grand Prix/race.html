The 2001 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 19, 2001 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 49 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2001 Hungarian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 94 points. Schumacher is 43 points ahead of David Coulthard.
