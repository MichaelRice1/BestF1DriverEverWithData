The 2001 Spanish Grand Prix started Apr 29, 2001 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mika Häkkinen next to him. David Coulthard started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 03 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and Jacques Villeneuve in third place.

After the 2001 Spanish Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 36 points. Schumacher is 8 points ahead of David Coulthard.
