The 2001 Monaco Grand Prix started May 27, 2001 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. David Coulthard started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 47 minutes and 22 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Eddie Irvine in third place.

After the 2001 Monaco Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 52 points. Schumacher is 12 points ahead of David Coulthard.
