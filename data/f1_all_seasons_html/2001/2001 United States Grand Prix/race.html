The 2001 United States Grand Prix started Sep 30, 2001 on Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Indianapolis, United States of America. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Mika Häkkinen next to him. Ralf Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 42 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2001 United States Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 113 points. Schumacher is 52 points ahead of David Coulthard.
