The 1977 Monaco Grand Prix started May 22, 1977 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. John Watson started from pole position with Jody Scheckter next to him. Carlos Reutemann started from third place.

In 1 hour, 57 minutes and 52 seconds Jody Scheckter finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and Carlos Reutemann in third place.

After the 1977 Monaco Grand Prix, Jody Scheckter is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 32 points. Scheckter is 7 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
