The 1977 Canadian Grand Prix started Oct 9, 1977 on Canadian Tire Motorsport Park in Bowmanville, Canada. Mario Andretti started from pole position with James Hunt next to him. Ronnie Peterson started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 00 seconds Jody Scheckter finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Depailler in second and Jochen Mass in third place.

After the 1977 Canadian Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Lauda is 17 points ahead of Jody Scheckter.
