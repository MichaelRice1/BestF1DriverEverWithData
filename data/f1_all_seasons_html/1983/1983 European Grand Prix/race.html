The 1983 European Grand Prix started Sep 25, 1983 on Brands Hatch in Fawkham, United Kingdom. Elio de Angelis started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 45 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Nigel Mansell in third place.

After the 1983 European Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 57 points. Prost is 2 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
