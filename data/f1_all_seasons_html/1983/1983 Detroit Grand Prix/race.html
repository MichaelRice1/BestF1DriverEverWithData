The 1983 Detroit Grand Prix started Jun 5, 1983 on Detroit Street Circuit in Detroit, United States of America. René Arnoux started from pole position with Nelson Piquet next to him. Patrick Tambay started from third place.

In 1 hour, 50 minutes and 53 seconds Michele Alboreto finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Keke Rosberg in second and John Watson in third place.

After the 1983 Detroit Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 28 points. Prost is 1 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
