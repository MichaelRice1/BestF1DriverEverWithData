The 1983 German Grand Prix started Aug 7, 1983 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Patrick Tambay started from pole position with René Arnoux next to him. Andrea de Cesaris started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 10 seconds René Arnoux finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Andrea de Cesaris in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1983 German Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 42 points. Prost is 9 points ahead of Nelson Piquet.
