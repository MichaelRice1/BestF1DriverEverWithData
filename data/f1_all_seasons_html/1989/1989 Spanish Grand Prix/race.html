The 1989 Spanish Grand Prix started Oct 1, 1989 on Circuito de Jerez in Jerez de la Frontera, Spain. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 47 minutes and 48 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1989 Spanish Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Prost is 16 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
