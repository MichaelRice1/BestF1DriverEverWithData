The 1989 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 26, 1989 on Autódromo Internacional Nelson Piquet in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Riccardo Patrese next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 58 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Maurício Gugelmin in third place.

After the 1989 Brazilian Grand Prix, Nigel Mansell is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Mansell is 3 points ahead of Alain Prost.
