The 1989 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 27, 1989 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 54 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Nigel Mansell in third place.

After the 1989 Belgian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 62 points. Prost is 11 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
