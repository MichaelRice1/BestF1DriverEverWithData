The 1989 Mexican Grand Prix started May 28, 1989 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 21 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Michele Alboreto in third place.

After the 1989 Mexican Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 27 points. Senna is 7 points ahead of Alain Prost.
