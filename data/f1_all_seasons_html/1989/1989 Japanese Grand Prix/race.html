The 1989 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 22, 1989 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 06 seconds Alessandro Nannini finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Thierry Boutsen in third place.

After the 1989 Japanese Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 76 points. Prost is 16 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
