The 1960 Portuguese Grand Prix started Aug 14, 1960 on Circuito da Boavista in Porto, Portugal. John Surtees started from pole position with Dan Gurney next to him. Jack Brabham started from third place.

In 2 hour, 19 minutes and 00 seconds Jack Brabham finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Bruce McLaren in second and Jim Clark in third place.

After the 1960 Portuguese Grand Prix, Jack Brabham is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 40 points. Brabham is 7 points ahead of Bruce McLaren.
