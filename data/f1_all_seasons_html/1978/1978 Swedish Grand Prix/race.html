The 1978 Swedish Grand Prix started Jun 17, 1978 on Anderstorp Raceway in Anderstorp, Sweden. Mario Andretti started from pole position with John Watson next to him. Niki Lauda started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 00 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and Ronnie Peterson in third place.

After the 1978 Swedish Grand Prix, Mario Andretti is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 36 points. Andretti is 6 points ahead of Ronnie Peterson.
