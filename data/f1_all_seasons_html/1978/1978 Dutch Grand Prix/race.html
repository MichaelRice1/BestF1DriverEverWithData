The 1978 Dutch Grand Prix started Aug 27, 1978 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Mario Andretti started from pole position with Ronnie Peterson next to him. Niki Lauda started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 04 seconds Mario Andretti finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ronnie Peterson in second and Niki Lauda in third place.

After the 1978 Dutch Grand Prix, Mario Andretti is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 63 points. Andretti is 12 points ahead of Ronnie Peterson.
