The 1990 Spanish Grand Prix started Sep 30, 1990 on Circuito de Jerez in Jerez de la Frontera, Spain. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 48 minutes and 01 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Alessandro Nannini in third place.

After the 1990 Spanish Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 78 points. Senna is 9 points ahead of Alain Prost.
