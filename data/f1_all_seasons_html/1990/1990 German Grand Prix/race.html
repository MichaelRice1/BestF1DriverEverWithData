The 1990 German Grand Prix started Jul 29, 1990 on Hockenheimring in Hockenheim, Germany. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 20 minutes and 47 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alessandro Nannini in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1990 German Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 48 points. Senna is 4 points ahead of Alain Prost.
