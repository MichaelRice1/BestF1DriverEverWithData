The 1990 San Marino Grand Prix started May 13, 1990 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Riccardo Patrese started from third place.

In 1 hour, 30 minutes and 55 seconds Riccardo Patrese finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Alessandro Nannini in third place.

After the 1990 San Marino Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 13 points. Senna is 1 points ahead of Alain Prost.
