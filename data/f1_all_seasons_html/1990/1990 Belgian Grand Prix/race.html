The 1990 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 26, 1990 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 26 minutes and 31 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Gerhard Berger in third place.

After the 1990 Belgian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 63 points. Senna is 13 points ahead of Alain Prost.
