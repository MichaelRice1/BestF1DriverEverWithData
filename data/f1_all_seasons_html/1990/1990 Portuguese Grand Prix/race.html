The 1990 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 23, 1990 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Nigel Mansell started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Ayrton Senna started from third place.

In 1 hour, 22 minutes and 11 seconds Nigel Mansell finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1990 Portuguese Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 78 points. Senna is 18 points ahead of Alain Prost.
