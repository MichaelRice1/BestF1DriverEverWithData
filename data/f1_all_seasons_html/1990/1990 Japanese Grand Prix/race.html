The 1990 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 21, 1990 on Suzuka Circuit in Suzuka, Japan. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 36 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Roberto Moreno in second and Aguri Suzuki in third place.

After the 1990 Japanese Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 78 points. Senna is 9 points ahead of Alain Prost.
