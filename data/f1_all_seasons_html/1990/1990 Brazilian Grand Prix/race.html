The 1990 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 25, 1990 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Thierry Boutsen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 21 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Ayrton Senna in third place.

After the 1990 Brazilian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 13 points. Senna is 4 points ahead of Alain Prost.
