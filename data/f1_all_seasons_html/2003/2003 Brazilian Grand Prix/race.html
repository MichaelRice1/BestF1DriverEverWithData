The 2003 Brazilian Grand Prix started Apr 6, 2003 on Autódromo José Carlos Pace in São Paulo, Brazil. Rubens Barrichello started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 17 seconds Giancarlo Fisichella finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2003 Brazilian Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Räikkönen is 9 points ahead of David Coulthard.
