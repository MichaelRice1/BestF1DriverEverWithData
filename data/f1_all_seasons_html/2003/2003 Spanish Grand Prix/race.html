The 2003 Spanish Grand Prix started May 4, 2003 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 46 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2003 Spanish Grand Prix, Kimi Räikkönen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 32 points. Räikkönen is 4 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
