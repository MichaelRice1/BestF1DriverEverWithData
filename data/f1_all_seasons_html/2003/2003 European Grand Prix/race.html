The 2003 European Grand Prix started Jun 29, 2003 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Ralf Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 43 seconds Ralf Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Juan Pablo Montoya in second and Rubens Barrichello in third place.

After the 2003 European Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 58 points. Schumacher is 7 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
