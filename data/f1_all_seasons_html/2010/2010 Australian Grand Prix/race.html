The 2010 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 28, 2010 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 33 minutes and 36 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Robert Kubica in second and Felipe Massa in third place.

After the 2010 Australian Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 37 points. Alonso is 4 points ahead of Felipe Massa.
