The 2010 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 18, 2010 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 42 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Nico Rosberg in third place.

After the 2010 Chinese Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 60 points. Button is 10 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
