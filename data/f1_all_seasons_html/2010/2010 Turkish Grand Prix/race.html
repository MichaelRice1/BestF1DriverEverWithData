The 2010 Turkish Grand Prix started May 30, 2010 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Mark Webber started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 47 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2010 Turkish Grand Prix, Mark Webber is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 93 points. Webber is 5 points ahead of Jenson Button.
