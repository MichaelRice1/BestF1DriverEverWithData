The 2010 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 1, 2010 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Mark Webber next to him. Fernando Alonso started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 05 seconds Mark Webber finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2010 Hungarian Grand Prix, Mark Webber is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 161 points. Webber is 4 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
