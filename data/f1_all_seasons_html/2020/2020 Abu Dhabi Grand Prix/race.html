The 2020 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Dec 13, 2020 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Max Verstappen started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 28 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2020 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 347 points. Hamilton is 124 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
