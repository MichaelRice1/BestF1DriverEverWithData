The 2020 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 19, 2020 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Lance Stroll started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 12 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2020 Hungarian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 63 points. Hamilton is 5 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
