The 2020 Styrian Grand Prix started Jul 12, 2020 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Carlos Sainz Jr. started from third place.

In 1 hour, 22 minutes and 50 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2020 Styrian Grand Prix, Valtteri Bottas is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 43 points. Bottas is 6 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
