The 2020 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix started Nov 1, 2020 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 28 minutes and 32 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2020 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 282 points. Hamilton is 85 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
