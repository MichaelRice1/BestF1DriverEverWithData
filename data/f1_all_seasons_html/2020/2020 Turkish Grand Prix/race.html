The 2020 Turkish Grand Prix started Nov 15, 2020 on Istanbul Park in Istanbul, Turkey. Lance Stroll started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Sergio Pérez started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 19 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sergio Pérez in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2020 Turkish Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 307 points. Hamilton is 110 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
