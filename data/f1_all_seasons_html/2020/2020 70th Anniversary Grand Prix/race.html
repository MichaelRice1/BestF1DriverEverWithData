The 2020 70th Anniversary Grand Prix started Aug 9, 2020 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Valtteri Bottas started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Nico Hülkenberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 19 minutes and 41 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2020 70th Anniversary Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 107 points. Hamilton is 30 points ahead of Max Verstappen.
