The 2020 Tuscan Grand Prix started Sep 13, 2020 on Autodromo Internazionale del Mugello in Scarperia e San Piero, Italy. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 2 hour, 19 minutes and 35 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Alexander Albon in third place.

After the 2020 Tuscan Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 190 points. Hamilton is 55 points ahead of Valtteri Bottas.
