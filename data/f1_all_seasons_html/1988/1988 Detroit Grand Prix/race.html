The 1988 Detroit Grand Prix started Jun 19, 1988 on Detroit Street Circuit in Detroit, United States of America. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Gerhard Berger next to him. Michele Alboreto started from third place.

In 1 hour, 54 minutes and 56 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Thierry Boutsen in third place.

After the 1988 Detroit Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 45 points. Prost is 12 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
