The 1988 Australian Grand Prix started Nov 13, 1988 on Adelaide Street Circuit in Adelaide, Australia. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 53 minutes and 14 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1988 Australian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 90 points. Senna is 3 points ahead of Alain Prost.
