The 1988 Brazilian Grand Prix started Apr 3, 1988 on Autódromo Internacional Nelson Piquet in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 06 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Gerhard Berger in second and Nelson Piquet in third place.

After the 1988 Brazilian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Prost is 3 points ahead of Gerhard Berger.
