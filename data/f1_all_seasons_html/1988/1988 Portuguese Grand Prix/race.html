The 1988 Portuguese Grand Prix started Sep 25, 1988 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Alain Prost started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Ivan Capelli started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 40 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ivan Capelli in second and Thierry Boutsen in third place.

After the 1988 Portuguese Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 81 points. Prost is 5 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
