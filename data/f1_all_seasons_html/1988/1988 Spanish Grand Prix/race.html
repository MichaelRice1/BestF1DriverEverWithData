The 1988 Spanish Grand Prix started Oct 2, 1988 on Circuito de Jerez in Jerez de la Frontera, Spain. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 48 minutes and 43 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nigel Mansell in second and Alessandro Nannini in third place.

After the 1988 Spanish Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 84 points. Prost is 5 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
