The 1988 French Grand Prix started Jul 3, 1988 on Circuit Paul Ricard in Le Castellet, France. Alain Prost started from pole position with Ayrton Senna next to him. Gerhard Berger started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 37 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Michele Alboreto in third place.

After the 1988 French Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Prost is 15 points ahead of Ayrton Senna.
