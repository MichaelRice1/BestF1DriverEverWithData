The 1988 Hungarian Grand Prix started Aug 7, 1988 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Thierry Boutsen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 57 minutes and 47 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Thierry Boutsen in third place.

After the 1988 Hungarian Grand Prix, Ayrton Senna is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 66 points. Senna is 0 points ahead of Alain Prost.
