The 1985 Portuguese Grand Prix started Apr 21, 1985 on Autódromo do Estoril in Estoril, Portugal. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Keke Rosberg started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 28 seconds Ayrton Senna finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michele Alboreto in second and Patrick Tambay in third place.

After the 1985 Portuguese Grand Prix, Michele Alboreto is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 12 points. Alboreto is 3 points ahead of Alain Prost.
