The 1985 Austrian Grand Prix started Aug 18, 1985 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Alain Prost started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Niki Lauda started from third place.

In 1 hour, 20 minutes and 12 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Ayrton Senna in second and Michele Alboreto in third place.

After the 1985 Austrian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 50 points. Prost is 0 points ahead of Michele Alboreto.
