The 1985 Detroit Grand Prix started Jun 23, 1985 on Detroit Street Circuit in Detroit, United States of America. Ayrton Senna started from pole position with Nigel Mansell next to him. Michele Alboreto started from third place.

In 1 hour, 55 minutes and 39 seconds Keke Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Stefan Johansson in second and Michele Alboreto in third place.

After the 1985 Detroit Grand Prix, Michele Alboreto is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 31 points. Alboreto is 7 points ahead of Elio de Angelis.
