The 1982 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 13, 1982 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Didier Pironi started from pole position with René Arnoux next to him. Alain Prost started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 39 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Riccardo Patrese in second and John Watson in third place.

After the 1982 Canadian Grand Prix, John Watson is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Watson is 10 points ahead of Didier Pironi.
