The 1982 Detroit Grand Prix started Jun 6, 1982 on Detroit Street Circuit in Detroit, United States of America. Alain Prost started from pole position with Andrea de Cesaris next to him. Keke Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 58 minutes and 41 seconds John Watson finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Eddie Cheever in second and Didier Pironi in third place.

After the 1982 Detroit Grand Prix, John Watson is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 26 points. Watson is 6 points ahead of Didier Pironi.
