The 1982 Caesars Palace Grand Prix started Sep 25, 1982 on Caesars Palace in Las Vegas, United States of America. Alain Prost started from pole position with René Arnoux next to him. Michele Alboreto started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 56 seconds Michele Alboreto finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with John Watson in second and Eddie Cheever in third place.

After the 1982 Caesars Palace Grand Prix, Keke Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 44 points. Rosberg is 5 points ahead of Didier Pironi.
