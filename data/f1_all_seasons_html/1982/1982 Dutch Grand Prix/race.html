The 1982 Dutch Grand Prix started Jul 3, 1982 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. René Arnoux started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 03 seconds Didier Pironi finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Keke Rosberg in third place.

After the 1982 Dutch Grand Prix, John Watson is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Watson is 1 points ahead of Didier Pironi.
