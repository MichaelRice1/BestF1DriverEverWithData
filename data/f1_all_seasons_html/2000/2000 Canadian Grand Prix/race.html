The 2000 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 18, 2000 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with David Coulthard next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 12 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Giancarlo Fisichella in third place.

After the 2000 Canadian Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 56 points. Schumacher is 22 points ahead of David Coulthard.
