The 2000 European Grand Prix started May 21, 2000 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. David Coulthard started from pole position with Michael Schumacher next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 00 seconds Michael Schumacher finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mika Häkkinen in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 2000 European Grand Prix, Michael Schumacher is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 46 points. Schumacher is 18 points ahead of Mika Häkkinen.
