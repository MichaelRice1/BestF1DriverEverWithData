The 1976 Japanese Grand Prix started Oct 24, 1976 on Fuji Speedway in Oyama, Japan. Mario Andretti started from pole position with James Hunt next to him. Niki Lauda started from third place.

In 1 hour, 43 minutes and 58 seconds Mario Andretti finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Depailler in second and James Hunt in third place.

After the 1976 Japanese Grand Prix, James Hunt is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 69 points. Hunt is 1 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
