The 1976 Spanish Grand Prix started May 2, 1976 on Circuito del Jarama in Madrid, Spain. James Hunt started from pole position with Niki Lauda next to him. Patrick Depailler started from third place.

In 1 hour, 42 minutes and 20 seconds James Hunt finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and Gunnar Nilsson in third place.

After the 1976 Spanish Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 30 points. Lauda is 15 points ahead of James Hunt.
