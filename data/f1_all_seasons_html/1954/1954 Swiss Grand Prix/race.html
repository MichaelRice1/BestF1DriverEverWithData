The 1954 Swiss Grand Prix started Aug 22, 1954 on Circuit Bremgarten in Bern, Switzerland. José Froilán González started from pole position with Juan Manuel Fangio next to him. Stirling Moss started from third place.

In 3 hour, 00 minutes and 34 seconds Juan Manuel Fangio finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with José Froilán González in second and Hans Herrmann in third place.

After the 1954 Swiss Grand Prix, Juan Manuel Fangio is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 42 points. Fangio is 18.36 points ahead of José Froilán González.
