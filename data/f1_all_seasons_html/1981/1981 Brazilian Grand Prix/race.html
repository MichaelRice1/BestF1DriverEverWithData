The 1981 Brazilian Grand Prix started Mar 29, 1981 on Autódromo Internacional Nelson Piquet in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Carlos Reutemann next to him. Alan Jones started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 23 seconds Carlos Reutemann finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alan Jones in second and Riccardo Patrese in third place.

After the 1981 Brazilian Grand Prix, Alan Jones is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Jones is 0 points ahead of Carlos Reutemann.
