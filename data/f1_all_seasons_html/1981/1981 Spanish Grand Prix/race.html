The 1981 Spanish Grand Prix started Jun 21, 1981 on Circuito del Jarama in Madrid, Spain. Jacques Laffite started from pole position with Alan Jones next to him. Carlos Reutemann started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 35 seconds Gilles Villeneuve finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jacques Laffite in second and John Watson in third place.

After the 1981 Spanish Grand Prix, Carlos Reutemann is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 37 points. Reutemann is 13 points ahead of Alan Jones.
