The 1981 Dutch Grand Prix started Aug 30, 1981 on Circuit Park Zandvoort in Zandvoort, Netherlands. Alain Prost started from pole position with René Arnoux next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 22 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Alan Jones in third place.

After the 1981 Dutch Grand Prix, Nelson Piquet is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 45 points. Piquet is 0 points ahead of Carlos Reutemann.
