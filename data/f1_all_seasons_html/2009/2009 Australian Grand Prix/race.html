The 2009 Australian Grand Prix started Mar 29, 2009 on Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit in Melbourne, Australia. Jenson Button started from pole position with Rubens Barrichello next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 34 minutes and 15 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Jarno Trulli in third place.

After the 2009 Australian Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 10 points. Button is 2 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
