The 2009 Monaco Grand Prix started May 24, 2009 on Circuit de Monaco in Monte Carlo, Monaco. Jenson Button started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 44 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2009 Monaco Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 51 points. Button is 16 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
