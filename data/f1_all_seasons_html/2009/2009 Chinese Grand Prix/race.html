The 2009 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 19, 2009 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 57 minutes and 43 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Jenson Button in third place.

After the 2009 Chinese Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 21 points. Button is 6 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
