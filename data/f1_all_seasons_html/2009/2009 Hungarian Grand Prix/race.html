The 2009 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 26, 2009 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Fernando Alonso started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Mark Webber started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 23 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2009 Hungarian Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 70 points. Button is 18.5 points ahead of Mark Webber.
