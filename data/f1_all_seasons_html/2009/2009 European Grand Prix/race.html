The 2009 European Grand Prix started Aug 23, 2009 on Valencia Street Circuit in Valencia, Spain. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Heikki Kovalainen next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 51 seconds Rubens Barrichello finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2009 European Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 72 points. Button is 18 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
