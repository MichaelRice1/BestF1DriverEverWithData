The 2009 Malaysian Grand Prix started Apr 5, 2009 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Jenson Button started from pole position with Jarno Trulli next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 55 minutes and 30 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nick Heidfeld in second and Timo Glock in third place.

After the 2009 Malaysian Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Button is 5 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
