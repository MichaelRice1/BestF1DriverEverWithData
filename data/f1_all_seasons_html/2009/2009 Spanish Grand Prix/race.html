The 2009 Spanish Grand Prix started May 10, 2009 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Jenson Button started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Rubens Barrichello started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 19 seconds Jenson Button finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Rubens Barrichello in second and Mark Webber in third place.

After the 2009 Spanish Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 41 points. Button is 14 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
