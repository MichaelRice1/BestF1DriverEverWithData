The 2009 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 13, 2009 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Adrian Sutil next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 16 minutes and 21 seconds Rubens Barrichello finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2009 Italian Grand Prix, Jenson Button is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 80 points. Button is 14 points ahead of Rubens Barrichello.
