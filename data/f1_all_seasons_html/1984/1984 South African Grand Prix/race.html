The 1984 South African Grand Prix started Apr 7, 1984 on Kyalami Racing Circuit in Midrand, South Africa. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Keke Rosberg next to him. Nigel Mansell started from third place.

In 1 hour, 29 minutes and 23 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Alain Prost in second and Derek Warwick in third place.

After the 1984 South African Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 15 points. Prost is 6 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
