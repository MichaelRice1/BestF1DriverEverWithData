The 1984 Austrian Grand Prix started Aug 19, 1984 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Elio de Angelis started from third place.

In 1 hour, 21 minutes and 12 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nelson Piquet in second and Michele Alboreto in third place.

After the 1984 Austrian Grand Prix, Niki Lauda is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 48 points. Lauda is 3.5 points ahead of Alain Prost.
