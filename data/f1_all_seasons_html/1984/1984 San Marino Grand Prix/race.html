The 1984 San Marino Grand Prix started May 6, 1984 on Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari in Imola, Italy. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Keke Rosberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 53 seconds Alain Prost finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with René Arnoux in second and Elio de Angelis in third place.

After the 1984 San Marino Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Prost is 11 points ahead of Derek Warwick.
