The 1984 Canadian Grand Prix started Jun 17, 1984 on Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Canada. Nelson Piquet started from pole position with Alain Prost next to him. Elio de Angelis started from third place.

In 1 hour, 46 minutes and 23 seconds Nelson Piquet finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Niki Lauda in second and Alain Prost in third place.

After the 1984 Canadian Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 32.5 points. Prost is 8.5 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
