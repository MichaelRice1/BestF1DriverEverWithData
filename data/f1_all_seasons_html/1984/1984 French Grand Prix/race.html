The 1984 French Grand Prix started May 20, 1984 on Dijon-Prenois in Dijon, France. Patrick Tambay started from pole position with Elio de Angelis next to him. Nelson Piquet started from third place.

In 1 hour, 31 minutes and 11 seconds Niki Lauda finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Patrick Tambay in second and Nigel Mansell in third place.

After the 1984 French Grand Prix, Alain Prost is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 24 points. Prost is 6 points ahead of Niki Lauda.
