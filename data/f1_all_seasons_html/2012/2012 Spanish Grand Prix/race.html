The 2012 Spanish Grand Prix started May 13, 2012 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Pastor Maldonado started from pole position with Fernando Alonso next to him. Romain Grosjean started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 09 seconds Pastor Maldonado finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Fernando Alonso in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2012 Spanish Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 61 points. Vettel is 0 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
