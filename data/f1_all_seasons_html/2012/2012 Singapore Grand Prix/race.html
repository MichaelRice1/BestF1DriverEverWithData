The 2012 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 23, 2012 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Pastor Maldonado next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 2 hour, 00 minutes and 26 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2012 Singapore Grand Prix, Fernando Alonso is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 194 points. Alonso is 29 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
