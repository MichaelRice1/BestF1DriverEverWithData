The 2012 Korean Grand Prix started Oct 14, 2012 on Korea International Circuit in Yeongam, South Korea. Mark Webber started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 28 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Mark Webber in second and Fernando Alonso in third place.

After the 2012 Korean Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 215 points. Vettel is 6 points ahead of Fernando Alonso.
