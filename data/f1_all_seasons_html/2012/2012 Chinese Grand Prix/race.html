The 2012 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 15, 2012 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Michael Schumacher started from third place.

In 1 hour, 36 minutes and 26 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Jenson Button in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2012 Chinese Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 45 points. Hamilton is 2 points ahead of Jenson Button.
