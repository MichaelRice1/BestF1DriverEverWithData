The 2018 Mexican Grand Prix started Oct 28, 2018 on Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez in Mexico City, Mexico. Daniel Ricciardo started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 28 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2018 Mexican Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 358 points. Hamilton is 64 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
