The 2018 British Grand Prix started Jul 8, 2018 on Silverstone Circuit in Silverstone, United Kingdom. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 29 seconds Sebastian Vettel finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2018 British Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 171 points. Vettel is 8 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
