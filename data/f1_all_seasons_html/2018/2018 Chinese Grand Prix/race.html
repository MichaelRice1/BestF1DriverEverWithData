The 2018 Chinese Grand Prix started Apr 15, 2018 on Shanghai International Circuit in Shanghai, China. Sebastian Vettel started from pole position with Kimi Räikkönen next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 35 minutes and 36 seconds Daniel Ricciardo finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Valtteri Bottas in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2018 Chinese Grand Prix, Sebastian Vettel is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 54 points. Vettel is 9 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
