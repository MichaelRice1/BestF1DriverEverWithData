The 2018 Italian Grand Prix started Sep 2, 2018 on Autodromo Nazionale Monza in Monza, Italy. Kimi Räikkönen started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 16 minutes and 54 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Valtteri Bottas in third place.

After the 2018 Italian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 256 points. Hamilton is 30 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
