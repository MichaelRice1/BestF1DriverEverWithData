The 2018 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 25, 2018 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Valtteri Bottas next to him. Sebastian Vettel started from third place.

In 1 hour, 39 minutes and 40 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Max Verstappen in third place.

After the 2018 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 408 points. Hamilton is 88 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
