The 1951 Spanish Grand Prix started Oct 28, 1951 on Pedralbes Circuit in Barcelona, Spain. Alberto Ascari started from pole position with Juan Manuel Fangio next to him. José Froilán González started from third place.

In 2 hour, 46 minutes and 54 seconds Juan Manuel Fangio finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with José Froilán González in second and Nino Farina in third place.

After the 1951 Spanish Grand Prix, Juan Manuel Fangio is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 31 points. Fangio is 6 points ahead of Alberto Ascari.
