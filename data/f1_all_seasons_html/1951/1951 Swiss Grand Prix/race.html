The 1951 Swiss Grand Prix started May 27, 1951 on Circuit Bremgarten in Bern, Switzerland. Juan Manuel Fangio started from pole position with Nino Farina next to him. Luigi Villoresi started from third place.

In 2 hour, 07 minutes and 53 seconds Juan Manuel Fangio finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Piero Taruffi in second and Nino Farina in third place.

After the 1951 Swiss Grand Prix, Juan Manuel Fangio is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 9 points. Fangio is 3 points ahead of Piero Taruffi.
