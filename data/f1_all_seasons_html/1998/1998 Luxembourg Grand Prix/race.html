The 1998 Luxembourg Grand Prix started Sep 27, 1998 on Nürburgring in Nürburg, Germany. Michael Schumacher started from pole position with Eddie Irvine next to him. Mika Häkkinen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 14 seconds Mika Häkkinen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Michael Schumacher in second and David Coulthard in third place.

After the 1998 Luxembourg Grand Prix, Mika Häkkinen is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 90 points. Häkkinen is 4 points ahead of Michael Schumacher.
