The 2016 Hungarian Grand Prix started Jul 24, 2016 on Hungaroring in Budapest, Hungary. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Lewis Hamilton next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 40 minutes and 30 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Daniel Ricciardo in third place.

After the 2016 Hungarian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 192 points. Hamilton is 6 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
