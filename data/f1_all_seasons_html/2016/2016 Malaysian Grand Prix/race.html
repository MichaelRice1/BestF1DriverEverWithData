The 2016 Malaysian Grand Prix started Oct 2, 2016 on Sepang International Circuit in Sepang, Malaysia. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Max Verstappen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 37 minutes and 12 seconds Daniel Ricciardo finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Nico Rosberg in third place.

After the 2016 Malaysian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 288 points. Rosberg is 23 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
