The 2016 Austrian Grand Prix started Jul 3, 2016 on Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Nico Hülkenberg started from third place.

In 1 hour, 27 minutes and 38 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Max Verstappen in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2016 Austrian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 153 points. Rosberg is 11 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
