The 2016 Belgian Grand Prix started Aug 28, 2016 on Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in Spa, Belgium. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Max Verstappen next to him. Kimi Räikkönen started from third place.

In 1 hour, 44 minutes and 51 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Daniel Ricciardo in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2016 Belgian Grand Prix, Lewis Hamilton is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 232 points. Hamilton is 9 points ahead of Nico Rosberg.
