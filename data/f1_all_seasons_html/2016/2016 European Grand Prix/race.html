The 2016 European Grand Prix started Jun 19, 2016 on Baku City Circuit in Baku, Azerbaijan. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Sergio Pérez next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 52 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Sebastian Vettel in second and Sergio Pérez in third place.

After the 2016 European Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 141 points. Rosberg is 24 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
