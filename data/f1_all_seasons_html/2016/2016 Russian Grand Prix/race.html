The 2016 Russian Grand Prix started May 1, 2016 on Sochi Autodrom in Sochi, Russia. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Sebastian Vettel next to him. Valtteri Bottas started from third place.

In 1 hour, 32 minutes and 41 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Lewis Hamilton in second and Kimi Räikkönen in third place.

After the 2016 Russian Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 100 points. Rosberg is 43 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
