The 2016 Singapore Grand Prix started Sep 18, 2016 on Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore, Singapore. Nico Rosberg started from pole position with Daniel Ricciardo next to him. Lewis Hamilton started from third place.

In 1 hour, 55 minutes and 48 seconds Nico Rosberg finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Daniel Ricciardo in second and Lewis Hamilton in third place.

After the 2016 Singapore Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 273 points. Rosberg is 8 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
