The 2016 Spanish Grand Prix started May 15, 2016 on Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya in Montmeló, Spain. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 41 minutes and 40 seconds Max Verstappen finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Kimi Räikkönen in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2016 Spanish Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 100 points. Rosberg is 39 points ahead of Kimi Räikkönen.
