The 2016 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix started Nov 27, 2016 on Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Lewis Hamilton started from pole position with Nico Rosberg next to him. Daniel Ricciardo started from third place.

In 1 hour, 38 minutes and 04 seconds Lewis Hamilton finished the race in first place.The podium was completed with Nico Rosberg in second and Sebastian Vettel in third place.

After the 2016 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, Nico Rosberg is first in the Formula 1 World Championship with 385 points. Rosberg is 5 points ahead of Lewis Hamilton.
